What gets published

Publishing renders your reviewed content into the chosen format with consistent branding and accessibility baked in. A published output includes:

  • Formatted content — lessons, units, or full courses laid out for the target medium.
  • Embedded media — images, videos, captions, and transcripts where supported.
  • Navigation & structure — table of contents, sections, and page breaks.
  • Branding — consistent typography, color, and identity across every format.
  • Accessibility — alt text, reading-level checks, and semantic structure.
  • Distribution metadata — title, authorship, version, and standards tags.

🖨️ Print / PDF Print-ready formats (PDF, Printable Workbook, Teacher Resource Kit) use your browser's Print → Save as PDF for true offline distribution. Publishing is demonstrated client-side; reviewed content passes the editorial gate before release. All content is illustrative.
