Package contents

Each export is a complete, self-contained course package. A typical bundle includes:

  • Course structure — modules, units, and sequenced lessons with a table of contents.
  • Lesson pages — formatted HTML lessons with embedded media and accessible markup.
  • Assessments & quizzes — QTI-formatted question banks with answer keys and scoring.
  • Media assets — videos, images, captions/transcripts, and audio narration.
  • Interactive activities — dependency-free HTML modules that run inside the LMS.
  • Gradebook mapping — points, weights, and standards alignment metadata.
  • Manifest & metadata — IMS/SCORM manifest, learning objectives, and tags.
  • Instructor materials — teacher guide, rubrics, and facilitation notes.

🧪 Demonstration Export is demonstrated client-side — selecting a destination prepares a sample package in-browser. No data leaves your device; production exports run through the editorial gate before release. All content is illustrative.
