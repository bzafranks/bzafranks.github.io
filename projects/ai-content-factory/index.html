Platform Overview

Not an AI lesson generator — an enterprise content production platform

Educational organizations spend enormous time producing instructional materials and recreating the same content in multiple formats. The Factory orchestrates AI across a full production pipeline to generate complete, aligned, accessible instructional packages — while editors and instructional designers review and approve every asset.

The Challenge

Content production is slow, repetitive, and hard to scale

🔁 Repetitive Reformatting

The same content gets rebuilt as slides, workbooks, videos, and LMS packages — by hand.

⏳ Long Timelines

Producing a full instructional package can take weeks of coordinated specialist effort.

🎯 Consistency & Quality

Keeping alignment, accessibility, and branding consistent across formats is difficult at scale.

Benefits

From weeks to minutes — one prompt, a whole package
