Accommodations vs. Modifications — what's the difference?

Accommodations change how a student accesses the same grade-level content and demonstrates learning — extended time, read-aloud, chunked assignments — without changing what's being taught or the expected standard of mastery. Modifications change what is being taught or expected — a reduced-complexity text, fewer required standards, or a different grading scale.
