❓ Excused vs. unexcused absences — and how to report one

An excused absence is one the school already knows the reason for — like illness, a medical appointment, or a family emergency you've told us about. It doesn't count against Jordan's attendance in the same way. An unexcused absence is one we don't yet have a reason for on file. A tardy just means Jordan arrived after the bell.

If you know Jordan will miss a day, you can report it ahead of time below — it only takes a minute, and it helps the school mark the absence as excused right away.
