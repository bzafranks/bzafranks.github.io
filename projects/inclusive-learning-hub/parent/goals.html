💡 What is an IEP goal?

An IEP (Individualized Education Program) is Jordan's personal learning plan. Instead of general expectations, each IEP goal names one specific, measurable skill — so everyone (including you!) can see real progress over time, not just a grade. The percentage on each card below shows how close Jordan is to reaching that skill's target.
