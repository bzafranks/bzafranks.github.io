❓ What does "standards mastery" mean?

You may see a term like standards mastery in Jordan's records. It simply means how well Jordan has learned a specific reading or writing skill that schools track over the year — for example, finding the main idea in a story, or understanding a character's motivation.

A score of 80% or higher generally means a skill is considered solidly learned ("mastered"). Anything below that just means Jordan is still building the skill with support — which is completely normal and expected at this point in the year. It is not a letter grade.
