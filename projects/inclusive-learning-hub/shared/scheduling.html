🗓️ Scheduling

Weekly Schedule

One combined weekly calendar for classes, therapy and related services, paraprofessional support, and room bookings — switchable by student, teacher, therapist, paraprofessional, or room. Overlapping sessions are automatically flagged so staff can resolve conflicts before they cause a missed service.
