🌱 Our Approach: UDL + Tiered Support

Inclusive Learning Hub Academy grounds every intervention decision in Universal Design for Learning (UDL) — designing instruction with flexibility built in from the start, rather than retrofitting access after the fact — layered with a three-tier MTSS framework. Tier 1 universal supports (flexible representation, engagement, and expression for every learner) are strengthened, not replaced, by Tier 2 targeted small-group interventions and Tier 3 intensive, individualized supports (IEP/504-driven). The goal at every tier is the same: remove barriers before they become gaps, and keep every student — general education or inclusive learning support — progressing toward grade-level goals.
