🗂️ Curriculum-Authoring Workflow

Standards are mapped once at the district/programme level, then instructional designers build modules against them in the Course Builder (see the Grade 7 ELA flagship course). Every module ties back to a standard here, so the admin curriculum map, the teacher gradebook, and the student mastery report all stay in sync automatically — no separate spreadsheet to maintain.
