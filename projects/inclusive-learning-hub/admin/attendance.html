📋 Excused vs. Unexcused Absences

Absences are categorized as excused (documented illness, medical/therapy appointments, bereavement, or pre-approved family events) or unexcused (no documentation on file after 3 school days). Chronic absenteeism is flagged when a student's attendance rate falls below 90% over any rolling 4-week window, regardless of excusal status, per district policy — this triggers an automatic case-manager or counselor check-in.
