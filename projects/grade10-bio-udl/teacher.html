Accommodation Strategies

Built-In Supports by Need

Learner needBuilt-in support
Reading difficulty / dyslexiaRead-aloud, readable font, transcripts, glossary
English language learnerVisuals, glossary, simplified summaries, translation-ready text
Low visionText scaling, high contrast, alt text
Motor differencesKeyboard-operable activities; large targets
Attention / executive functionOne task per screen, chunked video, progress tracking
Advanced learnersExtension tasks and open investigations

Continuity

Substitute / No-Specialist Guide

  1. Open the lesson page; read the one-paragraph overview.
  2. Press ▶ Play on the AI video — it delivers the teaching.
  3. Have students complete the interactive(s), virtual lab, and knowledge check (all self-correcting).
  4. Collect the chosen "show your mastery" product for the regular teacher.

No Biology background required. The accessibility toolbar supports every learner automatically.
