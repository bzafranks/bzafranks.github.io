🏛️ Governance Model — three tiers of leadership governance

A single national strategy, delivered through regional units, enacted at the school level — with clear authority and feedback flowing in both directions.

🏛️ National Leadership Board
Tier 1
Sets policy, standards & funding. Owns the national strategy, accountability framework, and quality assurance regime.
🌍 Regional Implementation Units
Tier 2
Translate policy into regional plans. Run talent pools, succession, mentoring and local accountability against national indicators.
🏫 School-Level Leadership
Tier 3
Principals & school leaders apply standards in practice, lead improvement, and report performance data upward for review.

Governance flow is illustrative and intended to be adapted to each national context.

📋 Roles & responsibilities

BodyMandateAccountability
National Leadership BoardSet national leadership standards, policy, certification rules and the multi-year funding strategy.Reports to the Minister of Education; publishes an annual national leadership report.
Leadership AcademyDeliver preparation, certification and continuing executive development against national standards.Accountable to the Board for certification quality and graduate readiness.
Regional Implementation UnitsPlan and deliver pipeline, succession and mentoring within the region; manage local talent pools.Accountable to the Board for regional readiness and gap closure.
Quality Assurance OfficeIndependent review of standards, certification integrity and policy compliance.Reports directly to the Board and flags risks to the Minister.
School LeadersApply leadership standards, lead school improvement and develop emerging leaders.Accountable to regional units for school performance indicators.

Roles are illustrative and should be aligned to existing national governance structures.

🔍 Quality Assurance & Accountability

▸Standards & certification integrityStrong

Independent audit of certification decisions and standards application; sampling across all regions each cycle.

▸Performance accountabilityIn progress

Regional units report against a shared indicator set; results feed the national dashboard and the annual report.

▸Continuous reviewOn track

A scheduled policy review every two years tests relevance, funding alignment and equity of outcomes across regions.
