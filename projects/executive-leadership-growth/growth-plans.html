A year of leadership growth

An illustrative annual arc that weaves goals, professional learning, and milestones into a continuous growth journey.

  • Quarter 1 · Reflect & set
    Establish the focus — review 360° feedback and the self-assessment; set annual goals and a first 90-day plan.
  • Quarter 2 · Learn & apply
    Build competency — attend a leadership conference, begin a professional reading, and apply high-leverage practices with coaching.
  • Quarter 3 · Monitor
    Mid-year review — check milestones, gather portfolio evidence, and adjust the plan while there's still time.
  • Quarter 4 · Evidence & renew
    Show impact — collect evidence of growth, celebrate wins, and set the next cycle of goals.
