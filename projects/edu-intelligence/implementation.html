Implementation Guide · For the Bahamas Ministry of Education

From Pilot to National Platform

A phased, low-risk roadmap to stand up educational intelligence — with privacy, change management, and measurable ROI.

Executive Summary

The platform unifies existing school data into role-based intelligence and AI decision support. It runs on standard web infrastructure, integrates with current systems, and scales from a pilot district to the whole nation — improving decisions and equity while protecting student data.

Implementation Roadmap

Phased Rollout

  • Phase 1 · Foundation (Months 1–2)

    Confirm data sources, governance, and roles; integrate assessment & attendance feeds.

  • Phase 2 · Pilot (Months 3–5)

    One district; train teachers & leaders; validate dashboards and AI recommendations.

  • Phase 3 · Refine (Months 6–7)

    Tune analytics, briefings, and alerts with user feedback; finalize change management.

  • Phase 4 · Scale (Months 8–12)

    Expand district-by-district to national coverage with the ministry dashboard.

💻 Technology & Data Integration

  • Web-based; works on existing devices
  • Connectors for assessment, attendance, gradebook, curriculum tools
  • Secure pipeline; SSO & role-based access
  • Exportable reports; LMS-friendly

👩‍🏫 Professional Development

  • Role-based training (teacher → ministry)
  • "Using data in 10 minutes" routines
  • Data-meeting facilitation & coaching
  • Responsible-AI literacy for leaders

🔄 Change Management

  • Start with quick wins & champions
  • Co-design dashboards with users
  • Protect time for data routines
  • Celebrate improvement stories

🔐 Privacy & Governance

  • FERPA/COPPA-aligned; data minimization
  • Audit trails & permissions
  • Explainable, human-in-the-loop AI
  • Bias monitoring (see governance)

🎯 Expected Outcomes

  • Earlier, more targeted intervention
  • Consistent curriculum implementation
  • Better resource allocation & equity
  • Data-informed leadership at every level

💰 Return on Investment

  • Uses existing data & devices — low marginal cost
  • Reduces wasted spend on untargeted programs
  • Improves outcomes that matter nationally
  • One platform replaces fragmented reports
