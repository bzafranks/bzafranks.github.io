🧭 Leadership Performance Framework

Leadership Scorecard

A leadership performance framework that generates a Leadership Performance Score, growth trends, recommended actions, and benchmark comparisons. Rate yourself across eight research-aligned leadership domains, then generate your score. All figures are illustrative sample data.

📋 How the scorecard works

Rate each indicator from 1 (Rarely) to 5 (Consistently). The framework converts your ratings into a 0–100 Leadership Performance Score, ranks your eight domains, surfaces strengths, and recommends targeted growth actions against an illustrative benchmark of 74.

Scale: 1 = Rarely · 2 = Occasionally · 3 = Sometimes · 4 = Often · 5 = Consistently.

🔭 Visionary Leadership

I articulate a clear, compelling vision that staff can describe in their own words.

I translate the vision into measurable goals that guide daily decisions.

📚 Instructional Leadership

I conduct regular classroom walkthroughs and provide timely instructional feedback.

I use evidence of student learning to coach teachers and guide professional growth.

🤝 People Leadership

I recognize staff contributions and create genuine opportunities for voice.

I invest in relationships and act to support staff wellbeing and retention.

🏛️ Operational Leadership

I design systems and routines that protect teacher time and run smoothly.

I manage budget, facilities, and resources to maximize impact on learning.

🎯 Strategic Leadership

I align resources and effort to a few high-leverage priorities.

I monitor progress with data and adjust the plan when results stall.

🌍 Community Leadership

I build authentic partnerships with families and community stakeholders.

I communicate transparently and invite community input on key decisions.

💡 Innovation Leadership

I create safe space for staff to pilot, test, and refine new ideas.

I scale what works and retire practices that no longer serve students.

⚖️ Ethical Leadership

I make values-based decisions and explain my reasoning openly.

I act equitably and hold myself accountable to the same standards as others.

All data shown is illustrative sample data created for demonstration. This is a reflective leadership self-evaluation, not a clinical or personnel instrument.
