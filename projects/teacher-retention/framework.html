🧩 The Framework

The Teacher Retention Framework

Ten research-based pillars that, together, build high-retention, high-performing schools. Each pillar names the leadership behaviors that influence whether teachers stay — and explains how it contributes to retention. Click a pillar to see how it drives retention.

🎓 Built on the research

The framework operationalizes Dr. Franks' doctoral research, "The Influence of Leadership Behaviors on Teacher Retention," alongside Transformational Leadership, Organizational Commitment, Psychological Safety, and Employee Engagement theory. Read the research →

Research themes inform the pillars; scenarios and figures across the system are illustrative.

🌟

1 · Transformational Leadership

Inspiring vision, individualized support, and intellectual stimulation.

▸How it drives retention

Teachers commit to leaders who inspire purpose and invest in them individually — a core driver of staying.

🛡️

2 · Trust & Psychological Safety

A climate where teachers feel safe to take risks and speak up.

▸How it drives retention

Safety and trust are foundational; without them, engagement and commitment erode.

🏅

3 · Teacher Recognition

Authentic, frequent, specific recognition of great work.

▸How it drives retention

Feeling valued is among the strongest predictors of staying. Open Recognition →

📈

4 · Professional Growth

Visible growth pathways, coaching, and leadership opportunities.

▸How it drives retention

Growth keeps teachers challenged and invested. Open Growth →

🗣️

5 · Teacher Voice

Genuine influence over decisions that affect their work.

▸How it drives retention

Voice builds ownership and commitment. Open Voice →

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

6 · Collaborative Decision-Making

Shared, distributed leadership and transparent processes.

▸How it drives retention

Shared leadership multiplies capacity and belonging.

💬

7 · Effective Communication

Clear, two-way, frequent communication.

▸How it drives retention

Communication reduces uncertainty and builds trust.

🌱

8 · Supportive School Culture

Belonging, collaboration, and care.

▸How it drives retention

Culture is the felt experience teachers weigh when deciding to stay.

⚖️

9 · Workload Management

Protecting time and reducing unsustainable demands.

▸How it drives retention

Unmanageable workload is a leading driver of burnout and exit. Open Wellbeing →

🔄

10 · Continuous Improvement

Listening, measuring, and acting in cycles.

▸How it drives retention

A school that keeps improving keeps its people. Open Analytics →
