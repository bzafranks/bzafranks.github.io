# Teacher Retention & Workforce Excellence System
### An Evidence-Based Leadership System for Teacher Satisfaction, Commitment & Long-Term Retention

The second project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z.
Franks' portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/teacher-retention/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A comprehensive *workforce-excellence system* that translates **Dr. Franks' doctoral research on
leadership behaviors that influence teacher retention** into a practical implementation system —
helping leaders build high-retention, high-performing schools through the behaviors that make teachers
want to stay and thrive.

## 🌍 Teacher Retention Challenge
Teacher shortages are a worldwide challenge. Recruitment fills posts, but research shows **leadership,
culture, and support** have far greater long-term impact on retaining effective educators.

## 🔬 Research Foundation
Grounded in the doctoral study **"The Influence of Leadership Behaviors on Teacher Retention,"** with
Transformational Leadership, Organizational Commitment, Psychological Safety, Employee Engagement, and
Motivation theory. *Research themes inform every feature; specific figures and scenarios are
illustrative representations for demonstration, not verbatim results.*

## 🧩 Framework Philosophy
Ten research-based retention pillars — Transformational Leadership, Trust & Psychological Safety,
Recognition, Professional Growth, Teacher Voice, Collaborative Decision-Making, Effective
Communication, Supportive Culture, Workload Management, and Continuous Improvement.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Retention Framework** — ten interactive pillars, each tied to retention
- **Dissertation Research** — problem, purpose, questions, framework, themes, recommendations
- **Leadership Behaviors** — practical guides + a saved **Retention-Leadership Pulse** self-assessment
- **Teacher Wellbeing / Voice / Recognition / Professional Growth** — the people-leadership toolkit
- **Retention Analytics** — engagement, satisfaction, turnover trends, exit-interview themes
- **Early Warning Dashboard** — a **retention-risk calculator** + proactive intervention guidance
- **Leadership Action Planner** — a saved 90-day plan, retention-plan generator, reflection journal
- **Resources** — stay-conversation guides, pulse surveys, workload audits, templates
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js**, plus project-local **`style.css`**
("thriving workforce" teal-green + gold chrome: retention pillars, dissertation layout, people
analytics, risk calculator, dark mode) and **`script.js`** (engine: retention-leadership pulse,
retention-risk calculator, saved action planner & reflection journal, plan/intervention generators,
gauges & charts, command palette). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, no build step.
**Research-informed; school data and scenarios are illustrative.**
