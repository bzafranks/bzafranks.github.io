💼 Business & Entrepreneurship

Overview: launch and run ventures; manage, market, and lead. Careers: entrepreneur, manager, marketer, accountant. Electives: business, marketing, accounting. Certifications: bookkeeping, digital marketing. College: business, finance, management. Outlook: strong (SME growth). Future skills: communication, financial literacy, leadership. Sample project: launch a real student business → PBL Studio.

🏥 Health Sciences

Careers: nurse, allied health, paramedic, lab tech. Electives: biology, health science, anatomy. Certifications: first aid/CPR, CNA. College: nursing, public health, medicine. Outlook: high demand. Future skills: empathy, professionalism, critical thinking. Project: community health campaign.

🏝️ Hospitality & Tourism

Careers: hotel management, culinary, events, eco-tourism. Electives: tourism, culinary arts, customer service. Certifications: hospitality, food safety. College: hospitality management. Outlook: very strong (core Bahamian industry). Future skills: communication, service, cultural awareness. Project: design a tourism campaign.

🌊 Marine & Blue Economy

Careers: marine technician, fisheries, conservation, aquaculture. Electives: marine science, environmental science. Certifications: boating/diving safety. College: marine biology, environmental science. Outlook: growing (sustainability focus). Future skills: environmental responsibility, problem solving. Project: marine conservation initiative.

🏗️ Construction & Skilled Trades

Careers: carpenter, electrician, plumber, mason. Electives: technical drawing, building tech. Certifications: trade certifications, OSHA safety. College/apprenticeship: trade & technical programs. Outlook: strong, steady. Future skills: precision, safety, project management. Project: community improvement build proposal.

⚙️ Engineering & Manufacturing

Careers: engineering tech, CAD, production. Electives: physics, technical drawing, robotics. Certifications: CAD, manufacturing basics. College: engineering. Outlook: growing. Future skills: problem solving, innovation. Project: design a sustainable product.

🎬 Creative Media

Careers: graphic/video, content creator, designer. Electives: media arts, design, music. Certifications: Adobe, content creation. College: media, design, communications. Outlook: growing (digital). Future skills: creativity, digital literacy, storytelling. Project: digital marketing campaign.

💻 Information Technology

Careers: IT support, developer, network admin. Electives: computer science, networking. Certifications: CompTIA, Google IT. College: computer science, IT. Outlook: very strong. Future skills: digital literacy, problem solving. Project: build an educational app.

🤖 Artificial Intelligence

Careers: AI/data assistant, prompt & automation roles. Electives: data, computer science, AI literacy. Certifications: AI fundamentals, data basics. College: data science, AI, CS. Outlook: rapidly growing. Future skills: AI literacy, ethics, critical thinking. Project: build an AI-assisted tool → AI Hub.

🔐 Cybersecurity

Careers: security analyst, IT security support. Electives: networking, computer science. Certifications: Security+, cyber basics. College: cybersecurity, IT. Outlook: high demand. Future skills: digital citizenship, ethics, vigilance. Project: school cyber-safety plan.

📚 Education

Careers: teacher, early-childhood, training. Electives: psychology, communication. Certifications: tutoring, child care. College: education, child development. Outlook: steady demand. Future skills: communication, leadership, empathy. Project: peer-teaching workshop.

🌱 Environmental Sustainability

Careers: sustainability tech, renewable energy, conservation. Electives: environmental science, geography. Certifications: renewable energy basics. College: environmental science, engineering. Outlook: growing. Future skills: environmental responsibility, systems thinking. Project: campus sustainability initiative.
