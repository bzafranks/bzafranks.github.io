# Bahamas Career & Future Skills Academy
### A Digital Learning Ecosystem for Technical High School Diploma Pathways

Project #7 in the **Curriculum Innovation & Instructional Design Portfolio** of Dr. Barbara Z.
Franks. Built on the portfolio's shared design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/career-skills/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A career-connected learning ecosystem that prepares students for higher education, technical
careers, entrepreneurship, and the future workforce. It is a **prototype vision** designed to
**complement — not replace —** the Bahamas Ministry of Education's new **Technical High School
Diploma pathways**.

## 🎯 Educational Challenge
The future workforce needs durable human skills, digital and AI literacy, entrepreneurship, and
financial literacy — competencies traditional programs rarely teach explicitly. This platform
embeds future-ready skills into everyday learning and connects school to industry.

## 🌟 Future Skills Philosophy
Technical skills **plus** durable human skills. Learning is project-based, work-based, and
portfolio-driven, organized around a **22-competency future-skills framework** and **12 career
pathways**, with stackable micro-credentials as proof of competence.

## 🇧🇸 Alignment with the Technical High School Diploma initiative
The academy maps 12 pathways to technical-diploma tracks, weaves future skills across courses,
and adds an employer portal for work-based learning — supporting the Ministry's rollout while
preserving its curriculum.

## ✨ Platform Features
- **Future of Work** trends + a **22-competency Future Skills Framework** (with a self-assessment)
- **12 Career Pathways** with an interactive **interest matcher**
- **Career Readiness Curriculum** (Grades 10–12: Explore → Specialize → Launch)
- **Project-Based Learning Studio** (authentic, portfolio-producing projects)
- **AI & Digital Literacy Hub** and **Entrepreneurship Hub** and **Professional Skills Academy**
- **Digital Portfolio** (interactive builder) and **12 Micro-Credentials** (claimable)
- **Student, Teacher, and Employer dashboards** (incl. an employer portal)
- **Implementation guide**, consulting **case study**, and **reflection**

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js** (with the data-viz components and
accessibility toolbar). A project script **`career.js`** powers the career-interest matcher, the
digital-portfolio builder, micro-credential claiming, and the future-skills self-assessment.
Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, GitHub Pages. Interactive features and dashboards use
illustrative sample data for demonstration.

## ▶ Run Locally
Open this folder's `index.html`, or serve the repo root (`python -m http.server`).

## 🚀 Deployment
Part of the `bzafranks.github.io` user-site monorepo — every push to `main` republishes. Lives at
`/projects/career-skills/` and is registered in `assets/js/projects-data.js`.
