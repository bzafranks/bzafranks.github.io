Entrepreneur Flashcards

Value Proposition
The clear reason a customer should choose your product — the problem you solve.
Profit
Revenue minus costs — what's left after you pay for everything.
Target Market
The specific group of customers your business serves best.
Social Enterprise
A business created to solve a social or environmental problem.
