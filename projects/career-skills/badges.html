Micro-Credentials & Digital Badges

Prove It — One Skill at a Time

Stackable, evidence-based credentials students earn by demonstrating real competencies — recognized in the portfolio and by employers.

How a credential is earned

Each badge requires criteria met, evidence submitted to the portfolio, an assessment against a rubric, a portfolio artifact, and a short reflection. (Demo: tap "Claim credential" to simulate earning — saved on your device.)

The 12 Micro-Credentials

Claim Your Credentials

🤖AI Explorer

Evidence: AI-assisted project + responsible-use reflection.

🗣️Professional Communicator

Evidence: resume + recorded mock interview.

💡Entrepreneur

Evidence: business plan + pitch.

📋Project Manager

Evidence: a completed PBL project with milestones.

🧭Community Leader

Evidence: a community/service initiative + reflection.

💰Financially Literate

Evidence: a budget project + reflection.

🎨Digital Creator

Evidence: a published digital media piece.

🧠Critical Thinker

Evidence: an analysis/decision task + reasoning.

🚀Innovation Champion

Evidence: an original solution to a real problem.

🏝️Hospitality Professional

Evidence: a service simulation + certification.

🌊Marine Steward

Evidence: a marine conservation initiative.

🎓Future Ready Graduate

Capstone: earn 6+ credentials + complete the capstone & portfolio.

In a live deployment, credentials would be issued as verifiable Open Badges with shareable links.
