Career Readiness Curriculum

A Three-Year Journey to Career-Ready

A scope and sequence that grows students from exploration to specialization to launch — complementing the technical diploma pathways.

Scope & Sequence

Grades 10 → 12

Grade 10

Explore

  • Career exploration & interest matching
  • Foundational future skills
  • AI & digital literacy basics
  • Intro financial literacy
  • Start the digital portfolio
Grade 11

Specialize

  • Pathway electives & certifications
  • PBL projects in the pathway
  • Entrepreneurship & professional skills
  • Work-based learning / job shadowing
  • Build micro-credentials
Grade 12

Launch

  • Capstone project & internship
  • Resume, interview, networking
  • College/career application support
  • Portfolio showcase to employers
  • "Future Ready Graduate" credential
