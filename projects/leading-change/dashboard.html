Risk indicators

Select a risk to view illustrative mitigations.

▸Implementation timeline slipping (cohort A)High

Signal: Two milestones at risk; coaching capacity stretched.

Mitigation: Re-sequence non-critical tasks, protect coaching time, and secure quick wins to rebuild momentum before the next cohort.

▸Communication reach uneven across teamsMedium

Signal: Effectiveness at 68%; some teams under-informed.

Mitigation: Add a multi-channel cadence, equip middle leaders as message-carriers, and open a two-way feedback loop.

▸Change fatigue among staffMedium

Signal: Survey comments cite workload during rollout.

Mitigation: Stop low-value work to make room, celebrate progress publicly, and pace the change with protected time.

▸Sustainability planning not yet startedWatch

Signal: Embedding & succession milestone is downstream.

Mitigation: Begin defining the systems, routines, and leadership succession that will hold the change in place.
