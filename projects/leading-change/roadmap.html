Multi-Year Roadmap

From launch to embedded practice — a three-year arc of sequenced milestones.

  • Months 0–3 · Foundation

    Create the vision & build the coalition. Establish urgency, co-create the vision, form the steering group, and complete a readiness assessment.

  • Year 1 · Launch

    Engage and begin. Roll out the engagement and communication plans, deliver foundational professional learning, and secure visible quick wins.

  • Year 2 · Scale

    Implement with purpose. Extend practice across teams, remove barriers, monitor leading indicators, and adapt based on evidence.

  • Year 3 · Embed

    Sustain the change. Institutionalize new routines, align systems and hiring, plan leadership succession, and celebrate lasting results.

  • Ongoing · Continuous review

    Monitor & adapt. Maintain a review cadence with the dashboard, refresh priorities, and keep the roadmap living.
