Overview

Not a change-management model — a school transformation framework

Educational change is inevitable. Successful transformation is not — it requires a structured approach that leads people, not just programs. This framework helps leaders create urgency and vision, build capacity, engage stakeholders, implement with purpose, monitor and adapt, and sustain change in the culture.

Why Change Initiatives Fail

Programs change; people transform

📦 Program, Not People

Leaders launch initiatives without leading the human side of change.

🗣️ Weak Communication

Unclear "why" breeds confusion, rumor, and quiet resistance.

🌫️ No Sustainability

Change isn't embedded in culture, so it fades when attention moves on.

Research Foundation

Grounded in proven change-leadership research

The framework integrates Kotter's Eight-Step Change Model, Prosci ADKAR, Transformational Leadership, Systems Thinking, Organizational Development, and educational-change research — with a through-line to Dr. Franks' doctoral work on the leadership behaviors that sustain people through change.
