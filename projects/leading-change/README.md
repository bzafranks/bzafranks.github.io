# Leading Change
### A Comprehensive Framework for Leading Sustainable School Transformation

A project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z. Franks'
portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/leading-change/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A complete *school transformation* framework — not just a change-management model. It helps leaders lead
the human side of change through six integrated phases, aligning leadership, communication, stakeholder
engagement, culture, and continuous improvement so change is embraced, sustained, and embedded.

## 🎯 The Educational Change Challenge
Most initiatives fail because leaders implement programs instead of leading people through change. This
framework focuses on the people, processes, and purpose that make transformation stick.

## 🧭 Framework Philosophy
Six phases: **Create the Vision, Build Leadership Capacity, Engage Stakeholders, Implement with Purpose,
Monitor & Adapt, and Sustain the Change.**

## 🔬 Research Foundation
Kotter's Eight-Step Change Model, Prosci ADKAR, Transformational Leadership, Systems Thinking,
Organizational Development, and educational-change research — with a through-line to Dr. Franks' doctoral
work on the leadership behaviors that carry people through change. *Transformation scenarios are illustrative.*

## ✨ Key Features
- **Change Framework** — six-phase explorer + the transformation journey
- **Readiness Assessment** — a scored diagnostic across eight readiness dimensions
- **Transformation Roadmap** — a saved planner + a change-action plan generator + multi-year timeline
- **Stakeholder Engagement** — an influence/interest map, engagement strategies, resistance management
- **Communication Strategy** — a saved planner + a message generator + communication calendar
- **Implementation Toolkit** — action plans, checklists, project tracker, risk register
- **Culture & Sustainability** — embedding change + a reflection journal
- **Leadership Dashboard** — readiness, progress, engagement, and risk at a glance
- **Resources** — readiness toolkit, roadmap & communication templates, sustainability playbook
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar
