# Strategic Planning Blueprint
### A Comprehensive Framework for Designing, Executing & Monitoring High-Impact Educational Strategies

A project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z. Franks'
portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/strategic-planning/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A complete *strategic management system* — not a planning template. It aligns leadership, people,
resources, operations, performance metrics, and continuous improvement so vision becomes measurable
results: from environmental scan to vision, priorities, KPIs, implementation, dashboards, and governance.

## 🎯 The Strategic Planning Challenge
Many plans are written but never implemented or monitored. This blueprint keeps strategy alive all year
through KPIs, a balanced scorecard, dashboards, and a governance rhythm.

## 🧭 Framework Philosophy
Six integrated stages: **Discover, Define, Design, Deploy, Measure, Refine.**

## 🔬 Research Foundation
Strategic Management theory, the Balanced Scorecard, Systems Thinking, Organizational Effectiveness, and
continuous-improvement research — with a through-line to Dr. Franks' doctoral work on the leadership
behaviors that sustain organizational performance. *Strategic plans & data are illustrative.*

## ✨ Key Features
- **Planning Framework** — six-stage explorer + the strategy cycle
- **Vision Builder** — an interactive vision-statement generator (saved) + graduate profile & themes
- **Environmental Analysis** — saved SWOT & PESTLE builders + stakeholder & risk tables
- **Strategic Priorities** — a priority matrix + saved strategic plan + initiative-brief generator
- **Goal & KPI Manager** — an **interactive KPI/goal tracker** (add, progress, persist) + balanced scorecard + OKRs
- **Implementation Roadmap** — multi-year timeline, initiatives, quarterly reviews
- **Performance Dashboard** — strategic goal completion, KPI progress, organizational health
- **Governance & Accountability** — saved governance planner, governance calendar, decision log
- **Resources** — templates for every stage + a reading list
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js**, plus project-local **`style.css`**
("strategic excellence" emerald + gold chrome: planning-stage cards, vision builder, SWOT/PESTLE,
priority matrix, balanced scorecard, KPI tracker, dark mode) and **`script.js`** (engine: vision &
strategy generators, saved SWOT/PESTLE/strategic-plan/governance forms, an interactive KPI/goal tracker,
gauges & charts, command palette). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, no build step. **Data illustrative.**
