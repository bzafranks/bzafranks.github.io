🧭 The Framework

Six Integrated Planning Stages

Strategy is a disciplined cycle, not a one-time retreat. These six stages take an organization from insight to vision to execution to renewal. Each names its purpose, leadership responsibilities, planning activities, deliverables, performance indicators, and review process. Click a stage to explore it.

Research Foundation

Synthesizes Strategic Management theory, the Balanced Scorecard, Systems Thinking, and continuous-improvement research — aligning vision, people, resources, and performance. Scenarios are illustrative.

1

Discover

Purpose: Understand the current reality and context.

▸Explore

Leadership: commission an honest scan; engage stakeholders.

Activities: SWOT, PESTLE, stakeholder & needs analysis.

Deliverables: environmental scan report.

Indicators: evidence breadth & stakeholder input.

Outcome: a shared, honest picture. Run the analysis →

2

Define

Purpose: Set vision, mission, and values.

▸Explore

Leadership: co-create an inspiring direction.

Activities: vision, mission, values, graduate profile.

Deliverables: vision & mission statements.

Indicators: clarity & shared ownership.

Outcome: a compelling destination. Build the vision →

3

Design

Purpose: Set priorities, goals, and KPIs.

▸Explore

Leadership: choose few, high-leverage priorities.

Activities: priority matrix, SMART goals/OKRs, balanced scorecard.

Deliverables: strategic priorities & KPIs.

Indicators: focus & measurability.

Outcome: a measurable strategy. Set priorities →

4

Deploy

Purpose: Execute with clear ownership.

▸Explore

Leadership: assign owners; align resources; communicate.

Activities: initiatives, timelines, project governance.

Deliverables: implementation roadmap.

Indicators: on-time milestones.

Outcome: momentum & delivery. Open roadmap →

5

Measure

Purpose: Monitor KPIs and govern the work.

▸Explore

Leadership: review evidence; hold accountability.

Activities: dashboards, board reporting, decision logs.

Deliverables: performance dashboard.

Indicators: KPI progress.

Outcome: transparency & accountability. Open dashboard →

6

Refine

Purpose: Learn and adjust the strategy.

▸Explore

Leadership: reflect; adapt; renew the plan.

Activities: annual review; reprioritize.

Deliverables: refreshed strategy.

Indicators: evidence-based adjustments.

Outcome: a living strategy. Governance →
