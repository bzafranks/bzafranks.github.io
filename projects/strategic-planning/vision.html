From Vision to Identity

A vision only lives when it shapes who you graduate, what themes you pursue, and how the organization understands itself.

🎓

Graduate Profile

The durable competencies every learner should carry on the day they leave us — the human evidence of the vision.

  • Knowledgeable: deep literacy and numeracy across disciplines.
  • Self-directed: sets goals, manages time, reflects, and improves.
  • Collaborative: communicates, listens, and leads with empathy.
  • Principled: acts with integrity and contributes to community.
🧩

Strategic Themes

The three-to-five enduring focus areas that organize every priority and KPI under the vision.

Excellence in Learning People & Culture Belonging & Wellbeing Family & Community Stewardship & Sustainability
🪪

Organizational Identity

Who we are, in one breath — the through-line that connects mission, brand, and daily behavior.

  • Purpose: develop knowledgeable, principled leaders.
  • Promise: a personal, rigorous, joyful education.
  • Personality: warm, ambitious, evidence-driven.
Mission & Values

Pair your vision with a mission (how you act now) and values (how you behave along the way). Mission answers "what we do daily"; vision answers "where we are going." Use the generator above to draft all three together.
