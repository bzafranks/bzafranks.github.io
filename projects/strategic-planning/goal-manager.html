Goal & KPI Manager

Turn strategy into measurable commitments. Set SMART goals and OKRs, align them to a balanced scorecard, attach KPIs and targets, track milestones and evidence, and let the system report progress and status automatically. A disciplined measurement system keeps the whole organization focused on what matters.

Research Foundation

Built on the Balanced Scorecard (Kaplan & Norton), Management by Objectives, OKR practice, and SMART-goal research — pairing leading indicators (implementation) with lagging indicators (outcomes). Goals, KPIs, and data are illustrative.

Live KPI & Goal Tracker

Add the goals and KPIs you are tracking. Enter a current value and a target — the tracker calculates percentage progress, assigns an automated status (red / amber / green), and renders a progress bar. Your goals are saved privately in this browser.

Balanced Scorecard

Four perspectives keep strategy balanced — outcomes, process, people, and resources. Each perspective carries an objective, a representative KPI, and a target.

🎓 Student Outcomes

Objective: Raise achievement and growth for every learner.

KPI: % of students meeting grade-level growth targets.

Target 85%

📚 Teaching & Learning

Objective: Deliver consistently excellent, evidence-based instruction.

KPI: % of lessons rated effective in instructional walkthroughs.

Target 90%

👥 People & Culture

Objective: Develop, engage, and retain a high-performing team.

KPI: Staff retention & engagement index.

Target 92%

💵 Operations & Finance

Objective: Steward resources efficiently and sustainably.

KPI: Budget variance & on-time initiative spend.

Target ≤ 3%

OKRs — Objectives & Key Results

OKRs pair an ambitious, qualitative Objective with 2–4 measurable Key Results. Expand each to see how an objective becomes measurable.

▸Objective: Every student reads on or above grade level

KR1: Increase students meeting reading growth from 72% to 85%.

KR2: 100% of teachers trained in the new literacy framework by Q2.

KR3: Reduce the share of below-benchmark readers by half.

▸Objective: Build a thriving, stable professional team

KR1: Raise staff retention from 86% to 92%.

KR2: Achieve an engagement-survey score of 4.3 / 5 or higher.

KR3: 90% of staff complete an individual growth plan.

▸Objective: Partner meaningfully with families & community

KR1: Lift family-engagement participation from 48% to 70%.

KR2: Launch quarterly community feedback forums.

KR3: 80% of families report feeling informed and welcomed.

Why It Works

Pairing leading and lagging indicators across all four scorecard perspectives prevents goal myopia — improving one measure (e.g., test scores) without quietly harming another (e.g., staff wellbeing). Transparent, regularly reviewed KPIs sustain accountability and adaptive learning. Illustrative.
