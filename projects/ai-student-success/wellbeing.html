Most students report feeling connected — 78% express a strong sense of belonging and 91% can name a caring adult at school, both trending up. Belonging dips modestly in Grade 8 (69%), where stress themes cluster around assessments and sleep. A supportive, strengths-based response is suggested: protect advisory/check-in time, expand peer-mentor and club opportunities in Grade 8, and share universal stress-management and sleep resources with families.
