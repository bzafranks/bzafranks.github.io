Platform Overview

Identify earlier. Intervene sooner. Decide responsibly.

Schools collect rich data on attendance, assessments, assignments, behavior, engagement, and well-being — but it lives in separate systems. This platform integrates that evidence and layers AI that surfaces patterns and explains its reasoning, so educators can act before academic failure occurs. AI supports decisions; it never makes them.

The Challenge

Struggling students are too often identified too late

🧩 Fragmented Data

Attendance, grades, behavior, and well-being sit in separate systems — patterns stay hidden.

⏱️ Reactive, Not Proactive

Support often begins only after failure, when the window to intervene has narrowed.

🤝 Uncoordinated Support

Without a shared view, interventions are inconsistent and hard to monitor for effectiveness.

From scattered data to coordinated, equitable support

Responsible AI

Explainable, private, and always educator-led

Predictive analytics in education demand care. Every recommendation is transparent and explainable, well-being data is handled with privacy safeguards and consent in mind, and educators review and decide before any action. The platform is designed against over-reliance on AI.
