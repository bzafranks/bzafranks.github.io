In this fictional sample, 74% of students are meeting standard and trending up. The strongest academic risk signal is middle-grades math — operations and fractions mastery falls below 60% by Grade 8, and Math accounts for 34% of all missing assignments. ELA and Science are steadier. Recommended priorities: targeted small-group reteach for math operations/fractions in Grades 6–8, an assignment-completion routine for the highest-missing students, and continued growth monitoring.
