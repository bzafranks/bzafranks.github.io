Overview

Not a climate initiative — a comprehensive culture framework

Exceptional schools are built on exceptional cultures. This framework helps leaders move from hoping for a good climate to intentionally designing the trust, voice, recognition, collaboration, and belonging that make a school thrive — and that keep great teachers.

Why Culture Matters

Culture is the strongest lever leaders control

🤝 Retention

Trust, recognition, and belonging are among the strongest predictors of teachers staying.

🎓 Achievement

Collaborative, high-trust cultures lift instruction and student outcomes.

💪 Resilience

Healthy cultures sustain change, wellbeing, and continuous improvement.

Research Foundation

Built on organizational psychology — and doctoral retention research

The framework integrates Transformational Leadership, Organizational Culture theory, Psychological Safety, Positive Organizational Psychology, and PLC research. Woven throughout is Dr. Franks' doctoral research on leadership behaviors influencing teacher retention — showing how trust, communication, collaboration, recognition, and psychological safety shape healthy culture. Surveys & scenarios are illustrative.
