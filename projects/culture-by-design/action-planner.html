90-Day Implementation

Implementation roadmap

  • Days 1–14
    Listen: culture pulse survey + small-group listening sessions.
  • Days 15–30
    Model the behavior you want to see — visibility, transparency, follow-through.
  • Days 31–45
    Launch an authentic recognition routine and open a teacher-voice channel.
  • Days 46–60
    Act visibly on one piece of teacher input; protect time for collaboration.
  • Days 61–75
    Embed the new practices into meetings and routines.
  • Days 76–90
    Re-survey, share progress, and celebrate wins together.

Sample roadmap — adapt the phases to your school's calendar.
