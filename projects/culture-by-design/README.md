# Culture by Design
### A Research-Based Framework for Building Positive School Culture, Trust & Organizational Excellence

A project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z. Franks'
portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/culture-by-design/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A comprehensive *organizational culture* framework — not a climate initiative. It helps leaders move from
hoping for a good climate to **intentionally designing** the trust, psychological safety, voice,
recognition, collaboration, and belonging that make a school thrive and keep great teachers.

## 🎯 The School Culture Challenge
Culture shapes retention, achievement, wellbeing, and resilience — yet it's often left to chance. This
framework makes culture a deliberate leadership strategy.

## 🧭 Framework Philosophy
Six interconnected culture domains — **Trust & Relationships, Psychological Safety, Shared Vision &
Values, Collaboration & Teamwork, Recognition & Belonging, and Continuous Learning & Innovation.**

## 🔬 Research Foundation
Transformational Leadership, Organizational Culture theory, Psychological Safety, Positive Organizational
Psychology, and PLC research — woven throughout with **Dr. Franks' doctoral research on leadership
behaviors influencing teacher retention** (trust, communication, collaboration, recognition,
psychological safety). *Culture surveys & scenarios are illustrative.*

## ✨ Key Features
- **Culture Framework** — six-domain explorer + the culture-by-design cycle
- **Culture Assessment** — a scored 9-dimension diagnostic → Culture Health Score, strengths, roadmap
- **Trust & Psychological Safety** — a trust dashboard + practical trust-building practices
- **Teacher Voice** — a **live idea portal** (submit & persist) + voice structures
- **Recognition & Belonging** — a **live recognition wall** + programs + milestones
- **Collaboration & Community** — PLCs, mentoring, partnerships, shared leadership
- **Culture Analytics** — a School Culture Index, engagement, trust, and retention trends
- **Leadership Action Planner** — a saved 90-day culture plan + culture-action generator + journal
- **Resources** — culture survey bank, trust & recognition toolkits, templates
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar

(warm "belonging" rose-magenta + teal chrome: culture-domain cards, culture assessment, trust dashboard,

live recognition & teacher-voice feeds, saved action planner & journal, a culture-action generator,
gauges & charts, command palette). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, no build step. **Illustrative data.**
