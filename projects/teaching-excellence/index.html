Overview

Not a teacher-evaluation model — a comprehensive instructional leadership system

Teaching quality is the single greatest school-based influence on student achievement. This framework helps leaders build a culture of instructional excellence through systematic practices that continuously improve teaching and learning — coaching, feedback, professional learning, and data-informed leadership.

The Challenge

Improving teaching at scale is hard

🔎 Observation ≠ Improvement

Observations alone rarely change practice without coaching and follow-through.

🧩 Fragmented Support

Coaching, PD, curriculum, and data often operate in silos.

📈 Sustaining Growth

Schools need continuous-improvement systems, not one-off initiatives.

Research Foundation

Grounded in instructional leadership & the learning sciences

The framework integrates Instructional Leadership, Adult Learning Theory, Visible Learning, Instructional Coaching research, Professional Learning Communities, and continuous-improvement science — with a through-line to Dr. Franks' doctoral research on the leadership behaviors that retain and grow great teachers. Research informs every domain; classroom scenarios are illustrative.
