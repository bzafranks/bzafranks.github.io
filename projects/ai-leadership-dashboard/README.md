# AI School Leadership Dashboard
### An AI-Powered Decision Intelligence Platform for School Leaders

Project #11 in the **Curriculum Innovation & Instructional Design Portfolio** of Dr. Barbara Z.
Franks, part of the **AI for Education Innovation Suite**. Built on the portfolio's shared design
system; deployed at **https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/ai-leadership-dashboard/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
An enterprise-grade **executive decision-support system** for principals, school leadership teams, and
district administrators — **not** a static reporting dashboard. It consolidates fragmented school data
(academics, attendance, behavior, curriculum, staffing, PD, climate) into one command center, then
layers explainable AI that surfaces patterns, recommends actions, and drafts board-ready reports.

## 🎯 Educational Challenge
School leaders make hundreds of decisions weekly with information scattered across SIS, assessment,
and LMS systems — making trends hard to see and intervention slow. This platform unifies the data and
adds AI that turns it into timely, prioritized, actionable insight.

## 🧠 Leadership Intelligence Philosophy
**AI surfaces the insight; leaders make the call.** Every recommendation is explainable — the platform
shows the evidence behind each insight and frames AI as decision support, never an autopilot.
Governance, privacy, and human oversight are built in.

## ✨ Platform Features
- **Executive Dashboard** — school-performance index, KPI scorecards, gauges, trends, risk indicators
- **School Health Overview** — traffic-light snapshot across 10 domains + benchmarks
- **Instructional Leadership** — observation trends, look-fors, coaching priorities
- **Curriculum Implementation** — standards coverage, pacing, fidelity, gaps
- **Teacher Performance & Coaching** — growth, sample profiles, AI coaching priorities
- **Student Achievement** — growth, mastery, subgroup gaps, predictive bands
- **Attendance & Engagement** — chronic-absenteeism, heat maps, AI risk alerts
- **Behavior & School Climate** — incidents, SEL, restorative outcomes, climate signals
- **Staffing & Human Capital** — vacancies, retention, pipeline, succession
- **Professional Development** — PD hours, PLCs, micro-credentials, AI PD pathways
- **School Improvement Planning** — goals, milestones (timeline), PDSA cycle
- **AI Executive Assistant** — explainable leadership Q&A (insight → evidence → actions)
- **Reports & Presentations** — board/district/principal report generator
- **Settings** — appearance, integrations (roadmap), responsible-AI governance, implementation guide
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), floating action button, notification center, drill-downs,
  animated KPIs/gauges/charts, toasts, **dark mode**, and a built-in accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js** (components + accessibility toolbar), plus a
project-local **`style.css`** (executive chrome: command-center shell, KPI scorecards, gauges,
traffic-light indicators, drill-downs, notification center, dark mode) and **`script.js`** (engine:
explainable AI executive assistant, report generator, animated gauges/KPIs/charts, drill-downs,
command palette, notifications). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS, inline SVG charts, `localStorage`, no build step.
**All data is realistic fictional sample data; AI is simulated client-side. Nothing leaves the browser.**

## ▶ Run Locally
