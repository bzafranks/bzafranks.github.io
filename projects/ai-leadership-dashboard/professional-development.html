Triangulating achievement, observation, and coaching data, three learning pathways will close the highest-leverage gaps this term. Each is tied to a specific data signal so PD time targets where it matters most.

Pathway 1 — Proportional Reasoning Instruction (Grade 8 Math). Tied to the ▼6-pt math dip in two sections. 3 modules + 2 coaching cycles + a peer-observation round. Audience: Grade 8 math team and the first-year teacher.
Pathway 2 — Belonging & Restorative Practices (Grade 8). Tied to the dip in climate-survey belonging. 2 modules + advisory-curriculum norming + facilitator training to clear the restorative-circle backlog.
Pathway 3 — Checks for Understanding & Formative Assessment (Science PLC). Tied to below-target PLC participation and formative-accuracy gaps. PLC-embedded micro-learning + a shared assessment bank.
