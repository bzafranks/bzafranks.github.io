✨ AI Curriculum Recommendations Simulated

Curriculum is being implemented with strong fidelity overall (coverage 87%, consistency 79%). Two patterns warrant action: Grade 8 mathematics is ~3 weeks behind pace in two sections, and assessment alignment (73%) trails coverage — meaning some taught standards aren't being assessed as designed. Three focused moves:

  1. Re-sequence Grade 8 math pacing. Insert a one-week reteach block on ratios & proportions and a common re-assessment to recover the 3-week gap.
  2. Close the assessment-alignment gap. Map common assessments back to priority standards in ELA and Science, where alignment is weakest, before the next assessment window.
  3. Spread strong resource practice. The 84% resource-usage rate hides variation — have high-fidelity teams model their unit-resource routines for the two lagging departments.
