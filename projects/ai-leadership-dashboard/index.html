Platform Overview

Not a reporting dashboard — an executive decision-support system

School leaders make hundreds of decisions a week with information scattered across systems. This platform unifies academics, attendance, behavior, curriculum, staffing, and PD into one command center, then layers AI that surfaces patterns, explains its reasoning, and recommends actions — leaving the final call to the leader.

The Leadership Challenge

Fragmented data slows good decisions

🧩 Fragmented Systems

Data lives in separate SIS, assessment, and LMS platforms — making trends hard to see and intervention slow.

⏱️ Delayed Intervention

By the time reports are compiled, the window to act on attendance, behavior, or achievement risk has often passed.

🎯 Competing Priorities

Coaching, staffing, curriculum, and improvement planning all compete — leaders need to know what matters most, now.
