🏠 Suggested Home Activities

🗺️ Geography

Look at a map of The Bahamas together. Ask Andre to find your island and use the compass (N, S, E, W).

🏛️ History

Ask: "Who were the Loyalists, and what did they bring?" Let Andre teach you.

🎭 Culture

Talk about your family surname and settlement. Listen to Junkanoo music and name the instruments.

💰 Economics

At the store, spot foods grown or made in The Bahamas vs. brought in.

⚖️ Citizenship

Discuss one right and one responsibility Andre has at home.

🗂️ Portfolio

Ask Andre to show you a piece from the digital portfolio and explain it.

What "competency-based" means for you: Andre advances when he truly understands something — not just when time runs out. "Almost there" is good news: it means he's close and will get another chance to master it.
