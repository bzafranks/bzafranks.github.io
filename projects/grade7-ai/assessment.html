Adaptive Assessment System

Assessment That Adjusts to the Learner

Assessments aren't just measured — they respond. Difficulty adapts, results route students to the right path, and every score feeds the analytics that guide instruction.

The Assessment Stack

One Coherent System

🩺

Diagnostic

Establishes each learner's starting point and seeds the learner profile.

🔄

Adaptive Quizzes

Item difficulty adjusts to responses — fewer items, sharper signal.

✅

Mastery Checks

Gate progression: master it, then advance; if not, route to support.

🛠️

Performance Tasks

Authentic application scored with rubrics (the real evidence of learning).

🪞

Self-Assessment

Metacognitive checklists build ownership.

🤝

Peer Feedback

Structured "glow & grow" protocols.
