AI Coaches

A Thinking Partner, Not an Answer Key

Three embedded AI coaches scaffold student thinking with guiding questions, hints, and encouragement. Try them — each is interactive. Tap a prompt to see how the coach responds.

Design rule: the coach never simply gives the answer. It builds the student's reasoning, then hands the win back to them.

AI Learning Coach

General Tutor & Encourager

Under the Hood

How the Coach Scaffolds (Not Solves)

1 · Diagnose

Identifies what the student is really stuck on before responding.

2 · Question

Asks a guiding question or gives a hint that moves thinking one step.

3 · Hand back the win

Returns the final reasoning to the student and reinforces effort.

Implementation note: in production this is powered by a large language model with a carefully engineered system prompt (Socratic, grade-appropriate, safety-filtered) plus teacher-visible logs. The demo above runs scripted responses client-side. See the Implementation Guide for responsible-AI and data-privacy details.
