Overview

Great schools are built by great leaders

Leadership development in education is often fragmented, theoretical, and disconnected from the realities of leading schools today. The Academy unifies pathways, courses, coaching, simulations, and certification into one coherent ecosystem — grounded in adult-learning and leadership-development research, and applied to real leadership practice.

The Leadership Development Challenge

From fragmented PD to a coherent academy

🧩 Fragmented

One-off workshops rarely build durable leadership capacity.

📖 Too Theoretical

Leaders need practice, coaching, and feedback — not just content.

📈 Unmeasured

Growth needs pathways, competencies, and credentials to be visible.

Research Foundation

Grounded in adult learning & leadership-development research

The Academy applies Adult Learning Theory, Transformational & Instructional Leadership, Coaching research, Organizational Learning, and continuous-improvement science — with a through-line to Dr. Franks' doctoral research on leadership behaviors that influence teacher retention, demonstrating how leadership development improves organizational performance.
