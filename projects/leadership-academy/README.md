# Educational Leadership Academy
### A World-Class Professional Learning Ecosystem for Developing Transformational Educational Leaders

A project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z. Franks'
portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/leadership-academy/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A comprehensive leadership-development *ecosystem* — not a one-off PD program. It prepares aspiring
leaders, develops current leaders, and supports executives through personalized pathways, courses,
coaching, branching simulations, mentorship, certification, and a professional portfolio.

## 🎯 The Leadership Development Challenge
Leadership development in education is often fragmented, theoretical, and disconnected from the realities
of leading schools. The Academy unifies learning, practice, coaching, and credentialing into one system.

## 🧭 Academy Philosophy
**Learn → Practice → Coach → Apply → Certify.** Leadership is learned by doing — research-based content
paired with simulations, coaching, and authentic application, with growth made visible via competencies
and credentials.

## 🔬 Research Foundation
Adult Learning Theory, Transformational & Instructional Leadership, Coaching research, Leadership
Development theory, Organizational Learning, and continuous improvement — with a through-line to Dr.
Franks' doctoral research on leadership behaviors influencing teacher retention. *Courses and scenarios
are illustrative.*

## ✨ Key Features
- **Leadership Pathways** — 7 role-based journeys (aspiring → executive → ministry)
- **Competency Framework** — 8 interactive leadership competencies
- **Learning Academy** — featured programs, microlearning, masterclasses, progress tracker
- **Leadership Courses** — a 10-course catalog with saved completion tracking
- **Coaching & Mentoring** — executive coaching, a saved coaching planner + reflection journal
- **Leadership Simulations** — a working **branching scenario** (decisions → coaching feedback → outcome)
- **Certification Programs** — 6 competency-based credentials with claimable digital badges
- **Professional Portfolio** — saved leadership philosophy, goals, evidence, and credentials
- **Leadership Dashboard** — progress, competencies, certifications, and milestones
- **Resources** — a leadership library and tools
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js**, plus project-local **`style.css`**
(executive-education royal-purple + gold chrome: pathways, course catalog, certification badges,
branching-simulation UI, dark mode) and **`script.js`** (engine: the branching simulation player, saved
course/badge progress, saved portfolio/coaching planner & reflection journal, explorers, gauges & charts,
command palette). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, no build step. **Courses & scenarios illustrative.**
