Research note

High-impact leadership learning is job-embedded, sustained over time, and tied to authentic problems of practice rather than one-off workshops. Each course in this catalog therefore pairs concise modules with applied assignments and reflection, and maps to the competency framework and a pathway so learning connects directly to the leader's role. Courses are illustrative.

What's in Every Course

  • Overview — the purpose, audience, and pathway and competency alignment.
  • Learning outcomes — what you will be able to do by the end.
  • Modules — concise, sequenced lessons with video, readings, and microlearning.
  • Assignments — job-embedded tasks you apply in your own setting.
  • Assessments — knowledge checks and an applied capstone.
  • Resources — tools, templates, and a curated reading list.
  • Completion certificate — a credential added to your professional portfolio.
