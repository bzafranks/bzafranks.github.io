# Future-Ready Schools Framework

**Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite · Leadership Project #14**
A comprehensive leadership framework for **digital transformation, AI readiness, and educational innovation** — six strategic pillars, a future-readiness diagnostic, AI-readiness leadership, future skills, innovation culture, technology leadership, and a six-phase transformation roadmap. Bridges the Leadership Suite and the AI for Education Innovation Suite.

Part of the portfolio of **Dr. Barbara Z. Franks**, built on the shared portfolio design system.

🔗 **Live:** https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/future-ready-schools/

## The Future of Education Challenge

AI, digital technologies, and changing workforce demands are reshaping education. Many schools buy technology; few have a leadership strategy to prepare educators, students, and organizations for the future. This framework treats transformation as a **leadership challenge** — anchored in vision, capacity, culture, equity, and ethics.

## Framework philosophy

Six strategic pillars — **Visionary Leadership · Digital Transformation · AI Integration · Future Skills Development · Innovation Culture · Continuous Adaptation** — lead innovation responsibly while protecting instructional quality, equity, and student-centered learning.

> All figures are **illustrative sample data**. AI features are framed as decision-support that **assists — never replaces — educators**. The diagnostic saves locally via `localStorage`.

## Key features

- **Future-Readiness diagnostic** scoring six pillars → a Future-Readiness Index (Exploring → Leading).
- **Interactive digital-maturity model** — click to profile across dimensions.
- **Transformation/tech-plan generator** — digital transformation, AI readiness, future skills, innovation culture, technology investment, and 100-day launch plans.
- **AI-readiness dashboard** with responsible-governance framing, plus an innovation dashboard, future-skills matrix, and a six-phase roadmap.

## Technology stack

Vanilla HTML5 / CSS3 / JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step. Shared `../../assets/css/design-system.css` + `../../assets/js/portfolio.js`; project `style.css` (electric violet `#4b2fb3` + electric cyan `#18c2d4`, app-shell, dark theme) and `script.js` (future-readiness diagnostic, maturity model, plan generator, gauges, bars, count-ups, drill-downs, notifications, command palette, theme toggle). Persistence via `localStorage` (`frs:` prefix).
