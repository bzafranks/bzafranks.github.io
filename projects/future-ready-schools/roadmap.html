Roadmap timeline

  • Year 1 · Complete
    Phase 1 — Readiness Assessment. Baseline diagnostics across the six pillars; data, infrastructure, and culture audit.
  • Year 1–2 · Complete
    Phase 2 — Vision & Strategy. Shared future-ready vision, governance, and an outcomes-aligned investment roadmap.
  • Year 2 · Complete
    Phase 3 — Capacity Building. Sustained professional learning, coaching, and infrastructure readiness.
  • Year 3 · In progress

  • Year 5+ · Planned

📋 Phase detail — click each phase for milestones, responsibilities, KPIs & evaluation

▸Phase 1 — Readiness AssessmentComplete

Milestones: Future-readiness diagnostic completed; infrastructure & data audit; equity baseline; stakeholder readiness survey.

Leadership responsibilities: Sponsor the audit, secure honest baselines, and name a transformation lead and steering group.

KPIs: Diagnostic completion rate · baseline Future-Readiness Index · access-gap map.

Evaluation measures: Baseline data quality and stakeholder participation across all schools.

▸Phase 2 — Vision & StrategyComplete

Milestones: Shared vision co-created; governance & ethics charter adopted; multi-year investment roadmap aligned to outcomes.

Leadership responsibilities: Facilitate co-design with staff, students, and families; align budget and policy to the vision.

KPIs: Vision adoption & awareness · % investment tied to defined outcomes · governance charter ratified.

Evaluation measures: Strategy coherence review and stakeholder buy-in survey.

▸Phase 3 — Capacity BuildingComplete

Milestones: Job-embedded PD & coaching launched; professional learning communities established; infrastructure and access upgraded.

Leadership responsibilities: Protect time and funding for learning; model the practices; recognize growth and experimentation.

KPIs: PD completion & coaching reach · educator confidence · device/connectivity readiness.

Evaluation measures: Observation of classroom practice and capacity self-assessment.

▸Phase 4 — AI IntegrationIn progress

Milestones: Responsible-AI policy adopted; AI-literacy pathways for teachers & students; pilot use cases with human oversight.

Leadership responsibilities: Govern AI and data ethically and transparently; keep educators in control of decisions; monitor risk.

KPIs: Teacher & student AI literacy · governed initiatives · responsible-use incidents (target zero).

Evaluation measures: AI-governance audit and equity-of-access review for AI tools.

▸Phase 5 — Innovation ScalingPlanned

Milestones: Innovation labs and design-thinking routines spread system-wide; proven pilots scaled with fidelity; cross-school networks.

Leadership responsibilities: Resource the spread of what works; remove barriers; sustain recognition and shared learning.

KPIs: Initiatives scaled · participation across schools · sustained-practice rate.

Evaluation measures: Scaling fidelity review and impact-on-outcomes analysis.

▸Phase 6 — Continuous EvolutionPlanned

Milestones: Horizon-scanning and continuous-improvement cycles embedded; annual strategy refresh; sustainability and succession planning.

Leadership responsibilities: Institutionalize adaptation as a leadership habit; steward culture and renewal beyond any one leader.

KPIs: Improvement-cycle cadence · Future-Readiness Index trend · innovation pipeline health.

Evaluation measures: Annual future-readiness review and adaptive-capacity assessment.
