🧰 The Consultant's Library

Consulting Toolkit

The instruments a consultant reaches for at every stage of an engagement — organized around three moves: Diagnose, Design, and Deliver. Each instrument connects to a framework or template in the playbook. All examples are illustrative.

📋 How to use the toolkit

Start by diagnosing with audits, surveys, and self-assessments to establish a clear baseline. Then design the response with planning templates and the Transformation Blueprint. Finally, deliver with workshop agendas, checklists, and the executive reporting that keeps stakeholders aligned. Every instrument here is a sample created for demonstration.

▸Half-day strategy & alignment workshop · sample agendaDesign
  • 0:00–0:15 · Opening
    Welcome, purpose, and a shared look at the diagnostic findings.
  • 0:15–0:45 · Where we stand
    Walk the Transformation Readiness profile across the six dimensions.
  • 0:45–1:45 · Priorities
    Facilitated selection of three to five strategic priorities.
  • 1:45–2:30 · Roadmap
    Sequence priorities into a phased roadmap with milestones.
  • 2:30–3:00 · Commitments
    Owners, cadence, and next steps; close with the governance model.

Pair this agenda with the Transformation Blueprint to capture decisions in a living plan.
