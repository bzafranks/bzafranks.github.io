Curriculum Framework

A Coherent Architecture for the Whole Year

Eight integrated ELA strands, six standards-aligned units, and a consistent lesson structure that makes the curriculum portable across every delivery model.

Design Principles

What Holds the Curriculum Together

Backward Design

Every unit begins with the standards and desired evidence, then works back to learning experiences.

Gradual Release

"I do, we do, you do" is built into each lesson so independence is scaffolded, not assumed.

Universal Design for Learning

Multiple means of representation, expression, and engagement everywhere.

Spiraled Skills

Reading, writing, and language skills recur across units with increasing complexity.

Authentic Tasks

Each unit ends in a performance task that mirrors real reading, writing, and communication.

Delivery-Model Agnostic

Each lesson is designed to run face-to-face, live online, asynchronously, or hybrid.

Integrated, Not Isolated

Strands are woven through every unit rather than taught in separate silos.

🔤

🧠

Vocabulary

🗣️

Speaking & Listening

Discussion, collaboration, and presentation.

🎬

Media Literacy

Analyzing and creating across media formats.

💻

Digital Literacy

Researching, evaluating, and citing online sources responsibly.

🔍

Critical Thinking

Reasoning, inference, and argument across every strand.
