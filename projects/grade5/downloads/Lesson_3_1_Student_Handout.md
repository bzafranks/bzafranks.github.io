## Remember
- **Fact:** can be proven true.
- **Opinion:** what you think or believe.
- **Claim:** an opinion you will defend. A strong claim **takes a clear side**.
- **Claim Formula:** Topic + Position + Reason preview.

---

## Part 1 — Fact or Opinion?
Write **F** for fact or **O** for opinion.

1. ___ Our school day is six hours long.
2. ___ Recess is the best part of the day.
3. ___ Every student should learn an instrument.
4. ___ A triangle has three sides.
