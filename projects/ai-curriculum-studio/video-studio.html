Production Workflow

From script to publish — each generated video moves through a guided, AI-assisted pipeline with a required human-review gate before anything goes live. Tool names are illustrative integrations.

  1. 📝

    1 · Script

    AI drafts narration, scene beats, and on-screen text aligned to the objective.

  2. 🖼️

    2 · Slides

    Build the deck and visuals in Gamma or Canva from the generated outline.

  3. 🗣️

    3 · Avatar & Voice

    Add a presenter and narration with Synthesia or HeyGen; warm, mid-pace voice.

  4. 💬

    4 · Captions & Transcript

    Auto-generate synced captions and a full transcript for accessibility.

  5. 👤

    5 · Human Review

    An educator checks accuracy, standards fit, and pedagogy before approval.

  6. 🚀

    6 · Publish

    Export to the LMS or share via Adobe Express with captions and notes attached.

Teacher mode / Student mode

Switch the audience the generated video and notes target. Preview

Teacher mode surfaces presentation notes, pacing cues, and discussion prompts. Student mode trims to the on-screen lesson with captions and a self-check. Toggle is illustrative.
