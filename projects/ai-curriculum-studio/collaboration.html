Approval workflow

  1. Draft — author creates and self-checks the artifact.
  2. Peer review — a second author leaves comments and suggested edits.
  3. SME review — a subject-matter expert verifies accuracy and standards fit.
  4. Lead sign-off — the curriculum lead approves for release.
  5. Publish — the approved version is handed to the Publishing Center.
