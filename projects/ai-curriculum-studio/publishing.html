What gets packaged

Every publish bundles the full curriculum artifact set into the chosen format, with structure and accessibility preserved:

  • Course, unit, and lesson content with headings, navigation, and metadata
  • Assessments, rubrics, and answer keys
  • Teacher guides, student workbooks, and parent / family resources
  • Graphic organizers, vocabulary lists, and reference sheets
  • Standards alignment and a coverage manifest
  • Accessibility scaffolding — semantic headings, alt-text guidance, and a print-ready stylesheet
