# AI Curriculum Authoring Studio
### A Complete AI-Powered Platform for Designing Curriculum, Learning Experiences & Digital Instruction

Project #10 in the **Curriculum Innovation & Instructional Design Portfolio** of Dr. Barbara Z.
Franks, part of the **AI for Education Innovation Suite**. Built on the portfolio's shared design
system; deployed at **https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/ai-curriculum-studio/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A complete **curriculum authoring environment** — not a lesson generator. It takes curriculum teams
from concept to publication: standards alignment, scope & sequence, units, lessons, assessments,
rubrics, interactive media, AI video, and resources, with collaboration, versioning, templates, and
multi-format publishing — all in one professional SaaS workspace.

## 🎓 Educational Challenge
Curriculum development is time-intensive, fragmented across tools, and hard to keep aligned and
coherent at scale. This studio unifies the workflow and layers AI that drafts, aligns, and analyzes
while instructional designers and SMEs review and decide.

## 🧠 AI Curriculum Authoring Philosophy
**AI drafts and aligns; curriculum experts decide.** Every artifact is a draft for professional
review, moved through a transparent review-and-publish workflow. Pedagogical integrity, standards
fidelity, and human expertise are preserved throughout.

## ✨ Platform Features
- **Curriculum Workspace** — projects, drafts, published curriculum + NL studio assistant
- **Curriculum Builder** — drag-and-drop courses → units → lessons with vertical/horizontal alignment
- **Scope & Sequence Designer** — annual maps, pacing, progressions, calendars
- **Standards Alignment Studio** — map, search, crosswalk, gap analysis, coverage heatmap
- **Unit Planner / Lesson Builder** — full UbD units & gradual-release lessons
- **Assessment Studio / Rubric Generator** — all types, keys, Bloom's, DOK; analytic & holistic rubrics
- **Interactive Learning Builder** — HTML/CSS/JS activities with a live component preview
- **AI Video Studio** — scripts, storyboards, image prompts, full production workflow
- **Resource Generator** — teacher guides, workbooks, organizers, passages
- **Publishing Center** — wizard + export to HTML/PDF/Word/PPT/SCORM/Canvas/Classroom/Moodle…
- **Collaboration Hub** — kanban, comments, approvals, SME review, version history
- **Template Library** (13 pedagogy templates) · **Analytics** · **Settings**
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, autosave, dark mode, accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js**, plus a project-local **`style.css`**
(studio chrome: workspace shell, builder tree, scope & sequence grid, kanban, version history,
template gallery, publishing wizard, dark mode) and **`script.js`** (engine: simulated AI generators,
NL studio router, drag-and-drop builder, publishing wizard, library, analytics). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS,
`localStorage`, no build step. **All AI is simulated client-side; sample curriculum is illustrative.**
