Suggested home activities

  • Read together for 10 minutes, then ask one "tell me about it" question.
  • Talk through one real-life math moment (cooking, shopping, time).
  • Let Jordan "teach you" one thing learned today.
  • Celebrate effort and trying hard, not just right answers.

Short and steady beats long and rare — even a few minutes helps.
