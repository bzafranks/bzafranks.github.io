Platform Overview

Individualized planning for every learner — at a scale teachers can sustain

Every learner has unique strengths, interests, challenges, and goals. Creating individual learning plans by hand for a whole class is difficult. This platform synthesizes academics, attendance, strengths, interests, and assessment evidence into a draft plan with clear rationale — and keeps the educator firmly in charge of every decision.

The Challenge

Personalization is the goal — time is the barrier

⏳ No Time to Plan

Teachers can't realistically write individualized plans for 25–150 students by hand.

🧩 Scattered Evidence

Strengths, interests, assessments, and history live in different places and people's heads.

🎯 Hard to Sustain

Even great plans go stale without easy goal-setting, progress monitoring, and family updates.

Responsible AI

A planning assistant — not a decision-maker

The AI drafts and explains; educators review, approve, and adapt. Every recommendation shows why it was generated. Strengths and interests are illustrative planning inputs, never fixed measures of ability, and student data is treated with care and consent in mind.
