Progress-monitoring check-ins

A monitoring timeline shows how each intervention is reviewed on a regular schedule so the team can decide to continue, adjust, or change tier. Illustrative fictional data.

  • Week 1 — Baseline

    Baseline probes collected and goal set one step beyond current performance. Plan shared with family.

  • Week 2 — Check-in

    First weekly probe. Task-initiation data below trend line — flagged for a strategy adjustment.

  • Week 3 — Family update

    Mid-cycle update sent home; math and writing trending upward, fluency holding steady.

  • Week 4 — Data review (current)

    Team reviews trend lines against goals. Fluency moved to closer monitoring; math remains on track.

  • Week 6 — Formal review

    Compare baseline to trend; team decides for each plan: continue, adjust, or change tier. Outcome evaluation documented.
