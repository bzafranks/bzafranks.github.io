Pathway options

Each pathway sequences milestones at a pace matched to the learner, with progress visible at a glance. Fictional, illustrative data.

Foundational Support

Rebuild prerequisite skills with explicit, scaffolded instruction.

For learners who need to close foundational gaps before grade-level work, using concrete-representational-abstract sequencing and frequent feedback.

Milestones
  • Diagnostic + skill map (Weeks 1–2)
  • Targeted skill rebuilding with worked examples (Weeks 3–6)
  • Bridge to grade-level tasks with supports (Weeks 7–9)

Estimated timeline: ~9 weeks · Tier 2 small group, 3×/week

On-Level Progression

Steady movement through grade-level standards with flexible checkpoints.

For learners working at grade level, balancing direct instruction, guided practice, and choice to sustain engagement.

Milestones
  • Foundational review + diagnostic (Weeks 1–3)
  • Application & guided projects (Weeks 4–7)
  • Synthesis & transfer task (Weeks 8–10)

Estimated timeline: ~10 weeks · pace adjusts to progress data

Accelerated Learning

Compact mastered content and move ahead at a quicker pace.

For learners ready to move faster — pre-assessment unlocks the next unit, with depth added where interest is high.

Milestones
  • Pre-assessment & compacting plan (Week 1)
  • Advanced units with stretch tasks (Weeks 2–6)
  • Above-level transfer challenge (Weeks 7–8)

Estimated timeline: ~8 weeks · weekly mastery checks

Project-Based Learning

Learn standards through an authentic, sustained project.

For learners motivated by real-world products and choice; standards are embedded in an inquiry with a genuine audience.

Milestones
  • Driving question & project plan (Weeks 1–2)
  • Research, build, and iterate (Weeks 3–7)
  • Public product & reflection (Weeks 8–10)

Estimated timeline: ~10 weeks · milestone critiques weekly

Career Exploration

Connect learning to interests and real-world futures.

For learners building relevance — academic skills are anchored to career pathways, industry skills, and authentic experiences.

Milestones
  • Interest inventory & pathway scan (Weeks 1–2)
  • Project tied to an industry problem (Weeks 3–6)
  • Mentor connection or showcase (Weeks 7–8)

Estimated timeline: ~8 weeks · interest-aligned checkpoints

Gifted Extension

Deepen and extend with open-ended, high-challenge inquiry.

For identified gifted learners who need greater depth, complexity, and autonomy beyond grade-level expectations.

Milestones
  • Independent inquiry proposal (Weeks 1–2)
  • Expert-level investigation & product (Weeks 3–8)
  • Presentation to an authentic audience (Weeks 9–10)

Estimated timeline: ~10 weeks · mentor check-ins
