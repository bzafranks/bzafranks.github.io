Platform Overview

Not a chatbot — an integrated productivity platform for educators

This platform demonstrates how AI can act as an intelligent teaching assistant that automates repetitive work while keeping teachers as the instructional decision-makers. Every output is a draft for a professional to review, not a replacement for professional judgment.

The Educational Challenge

Teachers are drowning in tasks that pull them away from students

⏳ Growing Workload

Lesson planning, assessment creation, differentiation, documentation, and communication consume hours every week — most of it outside the school day.

🔁 Repetitive Tasks

Much of that work is repetitive and templatable — exactly what AI can accelerate, freeing time for relationships and instruction.

🧠 Decision Fatigue

Teachers make thousands of micro-decisions a day. AI can prepare strong first drafts so educators decide and refine, not start from blank pages.

Human-Centered AI

AI proposes. The teacher decides.

Every feature is built around Human + AI collaboration. The assistant drafts, suggests, and accelerates; the educator reviews, adapts, and owns every decision. Outputs are clearly labeled as drafts, and nothing is sent to students or families automatically.
