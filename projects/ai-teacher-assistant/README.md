# AI Teacher Assistant Platform
### An AI-Powered Productivity & Instructional Support System for K–12 Educators

Project #9 in the **Curriculum Innovation & Instructional Design Portfolio** of Dr. Barbara Z.
Franks, and the first product in the **AI for Education Innovation Suite**. Built on the portfolio's
shared design system; deployed at **https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/ai-teacher-assistant/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
An integrated AI productivity platform — **not a chatbot** — designed specifically for teachers. It
demonstrates how AI can act as an intelligent teaching assistant that automates repetitive work
(lesson planning, assessment creation, differentiation, parent communication, feedback, behavior
documentation, classroom management, and professional growth) while keeping educators firmly as the
instructional decision-makers.

## 🎯 Educational Challenge
Teachers spend countless hours outside the classroom on administrative and preparation tasks. Much of
that work is repetitive and templatable — exactly what AI can accelerate. This platform gives teachers
time back while improving instructional quality, modeling **Human + AI collaboration** rather than AI
replacement.

## 🤖 AI Product Philosophy
**AI proposes. The teacher decides.** Every output is a clearly labeled *draft* for a professional to
review, adapt, and own. Nothing is sent to students or families automatically. Responsible-AI
practices (human oversight, transparency, privacy) are built into the design.

## ✨ Features
- **AI Workspace** — a natural-language command center that routes requests to the right tool
- **Lesson Planner** — daily/weekly/unit plans with objectives, success criteria, hooks, exit tickets
- **Assessment Generator** — items with answer keys, Bloom's, and Depth of Knowledge alignment
- **Rubric Builder** — analytic & holistic rubrics with editable criteria
- **Differentiation Assistant** — adapts any activity for every learner (ELL, IEP, advanced, more)
- **Parent Communication Hub** — newsletters, positive notes, concern messages, invitations
- **Student Feedback Generator** — growth-minded, glow-and-grow feedback
- **Behavior Documentation** — objective, restorative-aligned reports and family summaries
- **Classroom Management** — seating, grouping, routines, icebreakers, morning meetings
- **PD Coach** — strategies, resources, reflection and PLC prompts
- **Prompt Library** (37+ favoritable prompts) · **Resource Library** (saved work, searchable)
- **Teacher Dashboard** — usage, time-saved metrics, weekly view, data visualizations
- **Settings** — appearance, default preferences, integrations (roadmap), responsible-AI & privacy
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), floating action button, toasts, **dark mode**, and a
  built-in accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js** (components + accessibility toolbar), plus a
project-local **`style.css`** (SaaS chrome: side rail, command palette, FAB, dark mode, generator
workspace) and **`script.js`** (the engine: simulated AI generators, natural-language workspace router,
resource library, prompt favorites, dashboard stats). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, no build
step, no dependencies. **All AI is simulated client-side for demonstration; no data leaves the browser.**

## ▶ Run Locally
Open this folder's `index.html`, or serve the repo root: `python -m http.server`.

## 🚀 Deployment
