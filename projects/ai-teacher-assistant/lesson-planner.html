AI Lesson Planner

Turn a topic or standard into a complete, ready-to-teach lesson in seconds. You set the inputs; the assistant drafts the structure so you can focus on the teaching.

Plan at any altitude — a single daily lesson, a weekly sequence, or a full unit arc. Each draft includes a learning objective, essential question, success criteria, a bell ringer, hook/engagement, an I-do / we-do / you-do mini-lesson, closure, homework, an exit ticket, and built-in extensions and interventions so every learner has a path forward.

Supports Elementary, Middle, and High School across every content area. Drafts are starting points — review and adapt for your students and standards.
