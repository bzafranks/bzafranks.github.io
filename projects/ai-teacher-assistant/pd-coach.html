Professional Development Coach

Your on-demand instructional coach. Name a focus area and a goal, and the assistant builds a personalized growth plan — high-leverage strategies, resources, and reflection — so you can keep getting better, one cycle at a time.

An AI instructional coach that recommends high-leverage strategies, resources, professional reading, videos, and micro-courses, then frames it all into actionable coaching goals, reflection prompts, PLC discussion ideas, growth plans, and observation prep. Each plan starts with a baseline, names one move to try, and closes the loop with evidence of impact.

Drafts are starting points — adapt recommendations to your context, learners, and school improvement goals.
