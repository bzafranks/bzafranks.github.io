Responsible AI & Data Privacy

This platform is built around human oversight. A few principles guide it:

  • All AI is simulated client-side. Every "AI" output in this demonstration is generated from templates in your own browser — there is no external model call.
  • No data leaves the browser. Anything you enter or save stays on this device in localStorage; it is never transmitted to a server.
  • Outputs are drafts. Generated lessons, assessments, messages, and feedback are starting points that require your review for accuracy, bias, and fit before use.
  • Teachers stay in control. The educator makes every instructional and grading decision; AI assists, it does not decide.

In a production deployment, this product would add a formal data-governance program, alignment with FERPA and GDPR, audit logs of AI-assisted actions, and explicit human-oversight gates before any output reaches students or families.

Data

This site stores the following entirely on your device: your saved resources (ats:library), favorited prompts (ats:favs), workspace conversation history (ats:history), time-saved stats (ats:stats), and your theme choice (ats:theme). Clearing it cannot be undone.
