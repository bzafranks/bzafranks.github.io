# Accreditation Excellence Framework
### A Comprehensive Quality-Assurance System for School Accreditation, Continuous Improvement & Institutional Excellence

A project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z. Franks'
portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/accreditation-excellence/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A complete *institutional quality-assurance system* — not an accreditation checklist. It unifies
standards, readiness, evidence, continuous improvement, and quality analytics so accreditation becomes a
byproduct of how the school operates every day, rather than a periodic compliance scramble.

## 🎯 The Accreditation Challenge
Schools too often assemble evidence in a panic each cycle, with artifacts scattered and accreditation
disconnected from improvement. This framework makes quality continuous and evidence always-ready.

## 🧭 Framework Philosophy
Six integrated quality domains — **Leadership & Governance, Teaching & Learning, Student Success,
People & Professional Capacity, Operations & Resources, and Continuous Improvement** — configurable to
any accreditation model (Cognia, CIS, WASC, Middle States, ISO).

## 🔬 Research / Quality-Assurance Model
Continuous Improvement theory, Quality Assurance principles, Organizational Effectiveness, and Systems
Thinking — with a through-line to Dr. Franks' professional experience leading accreditation initiatives,
quality reviews, and institutional effectiveness. *Standards & evidence shown are illustrative.*

## ✨ Key Features
- **Quality Assurance Framework** — six-domain explorer + the quality cycle
- **Accreditation Standards** — an expandable standards explorer + a self-study narrative generator
- **Readiness Assessment** — a scored 8-area diagnostic → readiness score, gap analysis, roadmap
- **Evidence Management** — an **interactive evidence tracker** (add, status, completion %, persisted)
- **Continuous Improvement** — saved improvement planner + generator + quality cycle + journal
- **Compliance Dashboard** — readiness, standards completion, evidence status, risk indicators
- **Quality Analytics** — a School Health Index, domain performance, and trends
- **Resources** — self-study, crosswalk, evidence inventory, and review templates
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

("quality seal" cyan-teal + bronze chrome: quality-domain cards, standards explorer, readiness
diagnostic, evidence tracker, compliance calendar, dark mode) and **`script.js`** (engine: the scored
readiness diagnostic, an interactive evidence tracker with completion stats, self-study & improvement
generators, saved improvement planner & journal, gauges & charts, command palette). Vanilla
HTML5/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, no build step. **Standards & evidence are illustrative.**
