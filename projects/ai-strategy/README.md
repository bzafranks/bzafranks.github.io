# Bahamas National AI Strategy for Education
### A National Vision for Responsible, Equitable, and Transformative AI Integration

Project #8 — the **capstone** — in the Curriculum Innovation & Instructional Design Portfolio of
Dr. Barbara Z. Franks (Instructional Leadership pillar). Built on the portfolio's shared design
system; deployed at **https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/ai-strategy/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A comprehensive **national education strategy** — not a curriculum project — showing how The
Bahamas could integrate AI across its education system to improve teaching, learning, leadership,
equity, efficiency, and workforce readiness, within a strong governance framework. It is designed
to resemble an interactive strategic framework suitable for review by a Ministry of Education and
international partners (UNESCO, CARICOM, IDB, World Bank, the Commonwealth).

## 🎯 Strategic Vision
"Every learner, educator, and school in The Bahamas has equitable access to safe, ethical, and
effective AI that enhances learning, strengthens teaching, improves decision-making, and prepares
students for the future workforce."

## 🧭 Purpose
Address AI across **policy, infrastructure, governance, professional development, curriculum,
assessment, data, ethics, equity, and implementation** — a systems-transformation project.

## ✨ Features
- **Vision & 12 strategic goals**; the case for AI and the national challenges it addresses
- **9 strategic pillars** (vision, objectives, initiatives, timeline, outcomes, metrics, stakeholders)
- **National AI Governance framework** (ethics, privacy, cybersecurity, procurement, risk)
- **Digital infrastructure** (cloud, identity, national LMS, data warehouse, offline Family-Island access)
- **Teacher PD** (4-level certification) and a **K–12 student AI-literacy progression**
- **Curriculum transformation** & **assessment innovation** (human oversight on high stakes)
- **Digital equity**, **partnerships & research**, **funding strategy**, and **M&E KPIs**
- **Interactive features:** an AI maturity self-assessment, an interactive 4-phase roadmap, and a
  scenario-planning tool — plus animated KPIs and data visualizations

Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js** (data-viz components + accessibility
toolbar). A project script **`strategy.js`** powers the AI maturity model, scenario-planning tool,
interactive roadmap phases, and animated KPIs. Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, inline SVG charts,
`localStorage`, GitHub Pages. All figures are illustrative for demonstration.
