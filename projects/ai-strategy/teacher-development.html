Teacher Professional Learning

Build Teacher Capacity at Scale

No strategy succeeds without teachers. A national AI professional-learning framework takes every educator from foundations to leadership — with certification and ongoing support.

Certification Pathway

From Foundations to Leadership

Level 1

AI Foundations

What AI is, responsible use, safety, and first classroom uses.

Level 2

AI Practitioner

Prompt engineering, AI lesson planning, feedback, productivity.

Level 3

AI Innovator

Assessment, differentiation, ethics deep-dive, peer coaching.

Level 4

AI Leader

School-wide implementation, mentoring, and change leadership.

What Teachers Learn

  • Foundational AI literacy & safe use
  • Prompt engineering for teaching
  • AI-assisted lesson planning & resources
  • Assessment & feedback with AI
  • Ethics, integrity & bias awareness
  • Productivity & workflow
  • Leadership & change management

How It's Delivered

  • Micro-credentials for each competency (stackable)
  • Coaching cycles & classroom support
  • Communities of practice & champion networks
  • Blended, self-paced + live (works on Family Islands)
  • Recognized national certification

Builds directly on the teacher-development model demonstrated in the Career & Future Skills and Grade 7 AI projects — scaled to the whole system.
