The Vision

"Every learner, educator, and school in The Bahamas has equitable access to safe, ethical, and effective AI that enhances learning, strengthens teaching, improves decision-making, and prepares students for the future workforce."

Executive Summary

Artificial intelligence is reshaping how the world learns and works. This strategy positions The Bahamas to harness AI on its own terms — improving teaching and learning, closing equity gaps (including for the Family Islands), modernizing assessment, and building a future-ready workforce — within a strong governance and ethics framework that keeps educators and students at the center.
