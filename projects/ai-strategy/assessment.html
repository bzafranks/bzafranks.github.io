Human oversight is non-negotiable

AI may assist with feedback, rubric drafting, and analytics — but high-stakes decisions (grades, promotion, certification) remain with qualified educators. This is codified in the governance framework.

Demonstrated in practice across the portfolio's adaptive checks, rubrics, and mastery systems (e.g., Grade 4 CBE, Grade 10 UDL).
