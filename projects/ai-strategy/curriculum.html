The guardrail

AI complements teachers and aligns with national curriculum goals. Educators set the objectives, review AI-generated materials for accuracy and bias, and make the professional decisions. AI saves time and widens options; it does not define what Bahamian students should learn.

Proven in this portfolio: the same AI-assisted design approach already powers the Grade 3, 5, 7, Grade 4 CBE, and Grade 10 UDL projects — this strategy scales it nationally with governance.
