Student AI Literacy

Every Graduate, AI-Literate

A K–12 progression that builds responsible, capable, future-ready users of AI — age-appropriate at every stage.

K–12 Progression

Growing With Students

Lower Primary (K–3)

Safe & Aware

  • Digital citizenship basics
  • What technology & "smart tools" are
  • Kindness & safety online
Upper Primary (4–6)

Curious & Critical

  • What AI is (and isn't)
  • Don't believe everything; verify
  • Protect personal information
Junior High (7–9)

Skilled & Ethical

  • Effective, responsible prompting
  • Evaluate & cite AI output
  • Academic integrity; bias awareness
Senior High (10–12)

Creative & Career-Ready

  • AI for research & problem solving
  • Creative & technical applications
  • AI in careers & the workforce
