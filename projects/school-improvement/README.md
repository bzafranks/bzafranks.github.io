# School Improvement Operating System
### An Enterprise Framework for Building High-Performing Schools Through Continuous Improvement

A project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z. Franks'
portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/school-improvement/**.

## 📘 Project Overview
A complete *operating system* for school improvement — not a plan that becomes a compliance document.
It aligns leadership, teaching, data, culture, resources, and strategy into one continuous cycle of
measurable improvement, with a School Health Index, strategic planning, action planning, monitoring,
and executive dashboards.

## 🎯 The School Improvement Challenge
Many improvement plans gather dust. True improvement is continuous, integrated, and evidence-driven —
how a school *runs*, not a binder filed once a year.

## 🧭 Framework Philosophy
Six integrated improvement domains — **Vision & Strategic Direction, Teaching & Learning, People &
Leadership, Culture & Community, Operations & Resources, and Performance & Accountability** — each run
through a PDSA continuous-improvement cycle.

## 🔬 Research Foundation
Continuous Improvement theory, Systems Thinking, Strategic Leadership, Organizational Development,
Educational Effectiveness research, Quality Assurance, and accreditation best practice — showing how
continuous improvement builds sustainable excellence rather than short-term compliance.
*School scenarios are illustrative.*

## ✨ Key Features
- **Improvement Framework** — six-domain explorer + the continuous-improvement cycle
- **School Diagnostic** — a scored assessment that generates a **School Health Index**
- **Strategic Planning** — vision/mission/values, an interactive **SWOT builder**, SMART goals, balanced scorecard
- **Data & Performance** — academic, attendance, climate, and operational dashboards
- **Action Planning** — a saved planner + an improvement-plan generator + initiative tracker
- **Monitoring & Evaluation** — quarterly-review generator, evidence checklist, goal monitoring
- **Continuous Improvement** — the full PDSA → Scale → Celebrate → Institutionalize cycle
- **Leadership Dashboard** — School Health Index, strategic priorities, risk indicators
- **Resources** — diagnostic toolkit, planning templates, protocols, library
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/⌘ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
Shared portfolio **design-system.css** + **portfolio.js**, plus project-local **`style.css`**
(Baldrige-style slate + gold chrome: improvement domains, diagnostic, SWOT builder, balanced scorecard,
PDSA cycle, dark mode) and **`script.js`** (engine: the scored School Health Index diagnostic, saved
SWOT/strategic/action planners & journal, improvement-plan & quarterly-review generators, gauges &
charts, command palette). Vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS, `localStorage`, no build step. **Scenarios illustrative.**
