🧭 The Framework

Six Integrated Improvement Domains

High-performing schools improve across six connected domains, run as one operating system. Each domain names its purpose, improvement objectives, leadership responsibilities, performance indicators, evidence sources, expected outcomes, and how it feeds the continuous-improvement cycle. Click a domain to explore it.

Research Foundation

Synthesizes Continuous Improvement theory, Systems Thinking, Strategic Leadership, Organizational Development, Educational Effectiveness research, and Quality Assurance / accreditation best practice. Scenarios are illustrative.

🧭

1 · Vision & Strategic Direction

Purpose: Set and sustain a clear, shared direction.

▸Explore

Objectives: co-create vision; align goals; communicate relentlessly.

Leadership: sets direction, aligns priorities.

Indicators: staff clarity, goal alignment.

Evidence: strategic plan, surveys.

Outcomes: coherent, focused improvement. Open strategy →

📚

2 · Teaching & Learning

Purpose: Improve instructional quality & student learning.

▸Explore

Objectives: raise teaching quality; close gaps.

Indicators: walkthrough trends, achievement, growth.

Evidence: observations, assessment data.

Outcomes: better teaching & results.

🤝

3 · People & Leadership

Purpose: Develop, support & retain great people.

▸Explore

Objectives: grow capacity; distribute leadership; retain staff.

Indicators: engagement, retention, PD.

Evidence: surveys, retention data.

Outcomes: strong, stable team.

🌍

4 · Culture & Community

Purpose: Build trust, belonging & partnership.

▸Explore

Objectives: strengthen climate; engage families.

Indicators: climate surveys, attendance, engagement.

Evidence: survey & participation data.

Outcomes: a positive, inclusive culture.

🏛️

5 · Operations & Resources

Purpose: Run efficient systems that enable learning.

▸Explore

Objectives: align resources to priorities; reduce friction.

Indicators: efficiency, resource use, tech readiness.

Evidence: operational data, budgets.

Outcomes: sustainable operations.

📊

6 · Performance & Accountability

Purpose: Use evidence to improve & be accountable.

▸Explore

Objectives: monitor progress; act on data.

Indicators: goal progress, the School Health Index.

Evidence: dashboards, reviews.

Outcomes: measurable, sustained gains. Open dashboard →
