School Diagnostic & Health Index

A comprehensive school health assessment that generates a single School Health Index. Rate each indicator honestly across the six improvement domains; the engine scores every domain, ranks strengths and priority areas, and points you straight into a strategic plan. Improvement starts with an unflinching, evidence-anchored picture of where the school stands today.

Research Foundation

Nine evidence areas — leadership effectiveness, teaching quality, student achievement, school culture, teacher engagement, community partnerships, operational effectiveness, resource management, and technology readiness — mapped onto six improvement domains. Grounded in Educational Effectiveness research, self-evaluation / accreditation practice, and Continuous Improvement theory. Scenarios are illustrative.

School Health Index

Rate each indicator

Use the five-point scale below. 1 = Not yet · 2 = Emerging · 3 = Developing · 4 = Embedded · 5 = Exemplary. Answer as many as you can — the index scores whatever you complete.

🧭 Vision & Strategic Direction

Evidence area: leadership effectiveness.

Leadership has set a clear, shared vision and staff can articulate the school's strategic priorities.

Decisions, goals, and resources are visibly aligned to the strategic plan rather than to competing priorities.

📚 Teaching & Learning

Evidence areas: teaching quality & student achievement.

Instruction is consistently high quality across classrooms, and walkthrough evidence shows effective, research-based practice.

Student achievement and growth data are strong, and achievement gaps between groups are closing over time.

🤝 People & Leadership

Evidence area: teacher engagement.

Staff are engaged, well supported, and report high morale; turnover is low and capacity is actively developed.

Leadership is distributed: teachers have meaningful voice, professional learning is purposeful, and talent is retained.

🌍 Culture & Community

Evidence areas: school culture & community partnerships.

The school climate is positive, safe, and inclusive; students and staff report a strong sense of belonging and trust.

Families and community partners are actively engaged as partners in learning, with two-way communication and shared goals.

🏛️ Operations & Resources

Evidence areas: operational effectiveness, resource management & technology readiness.

Daily operations run smoothly and efficiently; systems and processes reduce friction and protect time for learning.

Resources are managed strategically and aligned to priorities, and technology infrastructure is reliable and ready to support teaching.

📊 Performance & Accountability

Evidence area: evidence use across all nine areas.

The school regularly monitors progress against goals using reliable data, dashboards, and review cycles.

Evidence is acted upon: findings drive timely adjustments, and the team holds itself accountable for measurable results.

Scale: 1 = Not yet … 5 = Exemplary. Your responses stay private in this browser.
