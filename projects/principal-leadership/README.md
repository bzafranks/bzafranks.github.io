# Principal Leadership Excellence Framework
### An Evidence-Based Leadership System for Building Exceptional School Leaders

The first project in the **Educational Leadership & School Improvement Suite**, part of Dr. Barbara Z.
Franks' portfolio. Built on the shared portfolio design system; deployed at
**https://bzafranks.github.io/projects/principal-leadership/**.

## ≡ƒôÿ Project Overview
A comprehensive leadership *development system* ΓÇö not just a framework. It translates leadership
research and best practice into a practical pathway: understand six leadership domains, assess your
practice with an interactive diagnostic, plan your growth, lead with research-informed behaviors, and
reflect to sustain improvement.

## ≡ƒÄ» The Leadership Challenge
School leadership is among the strongest school-level influences on teacher effectiveness, culture, and
student success ΓÇö yet many principals receive limited preparation for leading complex organizations.

## ≡ƒº¡ Framework Philosophy
Six integrated leadership domains ΓÇö **Visionary, Instructional, People, Organizational, Community, and
Ethical** ΓÇö each defined by purpose, philosophy, core competencies, observable behaviors,
responsibilities, outcomes, and a five-level maturity pathway.

## ≡ƒö¼ Research Foundation
Grounded in **Transformational, Instructional, Distributed, and Servant Leadership** theory and
continuous-improvement science. Woven throughout is **Dr. Franks' doctoral research on leadership
behaviors that influence teacher retention** ΓÇö which weights the *People Leadership* domain (trust,
recognition, voice, support). *Research themes inform practice; scenarios and figures are illustrative.*

## Γ£¿ Core Features
- **Leadership Framework** ΓÇö interactive six-domain explorer + maturity pathway
- **Competency Model** ΓÇö expandable competency matrix (definition, behaviors, evidence, growth)
- **Leadership Self-Assessment** ΓÇö an 18-item diagnostic ΓåÆ leadership profile, readiness score,
  strengths, growth opportunities, recommended learning, and a personalized action plan (saved locally)
- **Development Planner** ΓÇö annual goals, a 90-day plan, a saved reflection journal, and a plan generator
- **Instructional Leadership** ΓÇö walkthroughs, feedback, coaching cycles, curriculum monitoring
- **Teacher Development** ΓÇö capacity, mentoring, career pathways (retention-focused)
- **School Culture** ΓÇö trust, psychological safety, climate dashboard
- **Strategic Leadership** ΓÇö vision ΓåÆ strategy ΓåÆ continuous improvement
- **Leadership Observation** ΓÇö growth-focused observation system + summary generator + rubrics
- **Leadership Dashboard** ΓÇö competency growth, engagement, climate, strategic priorities
- **Resources** ΓÇö protocols, templates, and a leadership library
- **Product UX:** command palette (Ctrl/Γîÿ + K), FAB, dark mode, accessibility toolbar
