Platform Overview

Not just a leadership framework — a complete development system

School leadership is among the strongest school-level influences on teacher effectiveness, culture, and student success — yet many principals receive limited preparation for leading complex organizations. This system transforms leadership research and best practice into a practical pathway: understand the domains, assess your practice, plan your growth, and lead measurable improvement.

The Leadership Challenge

Today's schools need prepared, supported leaders

🎓 Limited Preparation

Many leaders step into complex roles without a structured model for instructional and organizational leadership.

🔄 Teacher Retention

Leadership behaviors strongly shape whether teachers stay — yet retention-focused leadership is rarely taught explicitly.

📈 Sustainable Improvement

Schools need leaders who can drive change that lasts, not one-off initiatives.

Research Foundation

Grounded in leadership research — and in doctoral study of teacher retention

The framework integrates Transformational, Instructional, Distributed, and Servant Leadership theory with continuous-improvement science. Woven throughout is Dr. Franks' doctoral research on leadership behaviors that influence teacher retention — informing which competencies and behaviors the model emphasizes, especially in the People Leadership domain (trust, recognition, voice, and support).

Research themes are applied to inform practice; specific figures and scenarios shown across the site are illustrative.
