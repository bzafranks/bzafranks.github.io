Leadership Milestones

Key growth milestones across the leader's development year (illustrative).

    Baseline self-assessment complete. Strengths and growth areas identified across all six domains.
  2. Term 1
    90-day development plan launched. Focus set on People & Instructional Leadership.

    Coaching cycle underway. Walkthrough and engagement routines established; mid-year review in progress.
  4. Term 3
    Distributed-leadership expansion. Teacher-leader roles and shared decision structures rolled out.
  5. Term 4
    Re-assessment & renewal. Measure growth, celebrate progress, and set next-year priorities.
