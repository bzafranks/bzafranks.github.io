🧭 Your Leadership Journey

Growth is a cycle, not a checklist. Move through these stages, then begin again — each loop deepens your practice.

1

Understand

Learn the framework and competencies that define exceptional leadership.

2

Assess

Locate your current practice with the self-assessment.

3

Plan

Set a focused goal and a 90-day plan with the planner above.

4

Lead

Put high-leverage routines into daily practice and coach others.

5

Reflect

Review evidence, journal your learning, and start the next cycle.

Research Foundation

🎉 Celebrate milestones. Naming and celebrating progress — a completed cycle, a goal met, a teacher retained — sustains motivation and models the recognition culture that research links to engagement and retention. Mark each milestone before you begin the next loop.
