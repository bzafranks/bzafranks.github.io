📂 The Library

Leadership Resources

A curated library of practical tools that put the framework to work — protocols, templates, and toolkits organized by leadership competency. Each category gathers ready-to-adapt resources you can bring straight into your school.

Research Foundation

Every resource maps to a competency in the competency model and the six leadership domains. Tools in the People domain — recognition, teacher-voice, and wellbeing — are emphasized because Dr. Franks' doctoral research links those behaviors most strongly to teacher retention. (Resources are illustrative templates; research applied to inform practice.)
