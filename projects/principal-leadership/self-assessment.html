📝 Leadership Diagnostic

Leadership Self-Assessment

Rate yourself on 18 statements across the six leadership domains. The diagnostic generates your leadership profile, a readiness score, signature strengths, growth opportunities, recommended professional learning, and a personalized action plan. Your responses stay private in this browser.

Scale: 1 = Rarely · 2 = Sometimes · 3 = Often · 4 = Usually · 5 = Consistently

🔭 Visionary Leadership

I communicate a clear, shared vision that connects daily work to purpose.

I align school goals and decisions to our vision.

I keep the team focused and resilient through change.

📚 Instructional Leadership

I conduct frequent walkthroughs and give specific, growth-focused feedback.

I run coaching cycles and protect time for teacher collaboration.

I use evidence and data to drive instructional improvement.

🤝 People Leadership Retention-linked

I build trust and psychological safety; staff feel safe to take risks and speak up.

I recognize excellent work authentically and solicit and act on teacher voice.

I actively support teacher wellbeing and develop teacher leaders.

🏛️ Organizational Leadership

I design efficient systems and routines that protect teacher time.

I manage resources and workload to reduce burnout.

I make transparent, consistent operational decisions.

🌍 Community Leadership

I communicate proactively and build two-way relationships with families.

I build community partnerships that support students and staff.

I foster a welcoming, culturally responsive school climate.

⚖️ Ethical Leadership

I act with integrity and make values-based, equitable decisions.

I am transparent about my reasoning and follow through on commitments.

I safeguard equity and hold myself and others accountable.

This is a reflective self-diagnostic, not an evaluation. Domain weighting reflects research on leadership behaviors linked to teacher retention and school performance.
