Executive Summary

Profile

A research-informed education executive who designs complete learning systems — and the leadership and AI capacity to run them — so schools and education systems deliver rigorous, engaging, and equitable learning at scale and through change.

My work spans four levels: classroom & curriculum innovation, AI-powered education product design, school leadership & organizational improvement, and ministry / system-level transformation — connected by the conviction that education improves when leadership, instruction, technology, and research are designed to work as one system. I partner with ministries, districts, schools, universities, foundations, and edtech organizations as an executive advisor and designer.
