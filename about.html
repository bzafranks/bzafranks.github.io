About

Dr. Barbara Z. Franks

Senior education leader, instructional designer, AI-education strategist, researcher, and consultant — designing solutions for classrooms, schools, edtech companies, and national education systems.

I design complete learning systems — and the leadership and AI capacity to run them — so schools and education systems can deliver rigorous, engaging, and equitable learning at scale and through change.

My work spans four levels: classroom & curriculum innovation, AI-powered education product design, school leadership & organizational improvement, and ministry / system-level transformation — connected by a single conviction: education improves when leadership, instruction, technology, and research are designed to work as one system.
